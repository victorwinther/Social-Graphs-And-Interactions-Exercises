'''Chase Matthew McQuitty'''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ascap.com/repertory#/ace/search/workID/913719154 |title=CAN'T BUY TIME |website=ASCAP |publisher=American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers |access-date=May 15, 2024}}</ref> (born December 20, 1997) is an American [[country music]] singer and songwriter. Signed to [[Warner Records]] Nashville, he charted in late 2023 with the single "[[Love You Again]]".

==Biography==
Chase Matthew was born in [[Sevierville, Tennessee]], grew up in [[Nashville, Tennessee]] and began recording music in his teenage years while also working as a mechanic.<ref name="allmusic">{{cite web | url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/chase-matthew-mn0004105421#biography | title=Chase Matthew biography | website=[[AllMusic]] | accessdate=May 3, 2024 | author=Timothy Monger}}</ref> In 2021, [[country rap]] artist [[Upchurch (musician)|Upchurch]] heard some of Matthew's songs online and signed him to his Holler Boy label. Through Holler Boy, Matthew released the single "County Line" from the EP of the same name, followed by his debut album ''Born for This'' in 2022.<ref name="allmusic"/>

In October 2022, Matthew signed with [[Warner Records]] Nashville.<ref>{{cite magazine | url=https://www.billboard.com/pro/chase-matthew-signs-warner-music-nashville/ | title=Chase Matthew Partners With Warner Music Nashville | magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] | date=October 13, 2022 | access-date=May 3, 2024}}</ref> The label released the album ''Come Get Your Memory'', consisting of 25 songs, the following June. This was followed by the single "Love You Again".<ref>{{cite web | url=https://triblive.com/aande/music/country-singer-chase-matthew-talks-about-dream-come-true-and-his-faith-ahead-of-pittsburgh-show/ | title=Country singer Chase Matthew talks about dreams come true and his faith ahead of Pittsburgh show | website=Trib Live | date=August 16, 2023 | access-date=May 3, 2024}}</ref> On November 10, 2023, he released his second EP ''Hey World Sessions''.

On February 16, 2024, Matthew released his third EP ''We All Grow Up''.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://musicrow.com/2024/01/chase-matthew-to-remind-us-that-we-all-grow-up |title=Chase Matthew to Remind Us That 'We All Grow Up' |author=Liza Anderson |publisher=Music Row |date=January 19, 2024 |access-date=May 17, 2024}}</ref>

On June 18, 2024, Matthew released the song "Saltwater Cinderella" and announced his fourth EP ''Always Be Mine''. It was released on July 4, 2024. <ref>{{cite web |url=https://ruralradio.com/khyy/abc_news/chase-matthews-ready-with-the-spontaneous-always-be-mine-ep-abcid6193f76e |title=Chase Matthew's Ready with the Spontaneous 'Always Be Mine' EP |author=ABC News Radio |publisher=107.3 the Trail |date=June 18, 2024 |access-date=June 19, 2024}}</ref>
