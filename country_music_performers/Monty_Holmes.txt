'''Monty Holmes''' (born in [[Lubbock, Texas]]<ref name="whitburn">{{cite book|last=Whitburn|first=Joel|title=Hot Country Songs 1944 to 2008|publisher=Record Research, Inc|page=192|year=2008|isbn=978-0-89820-177-2}}</ref>) is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter. Holmes signed to [[Bang Records|Bang II Records]] and released his debut album, ''All I Ever Wanted'', in 1998.<ref name="amgbio">[{{AllMusic|class=artist|id=p294668|pure_url=yes}} Monty Holmes] at [[Allmusic]]</ref> His highest charting single, "Why'd You Start Lookin' So Good," peaked at number 43 in 1998.<ref name="whitburn" /> Holmes' songs have been recorded by [[George Strait]] ("[[When Did You Stop Loving Me]]," "[[I Know She Still Loves Me]]," "[[Troubadour (song)|Troubadour]]") and [[Lee Ann Womack]] ("[[Never Again, Again]]").<ref name="amgbio" />
