'''Redneck Souljers''' are an American [[country rap]] duo from [[Winchester, Tennessee]], composed of rappers Wesley "Fatt Tarr" Alonso and Christopher "C-Hubb" Walls. They were formed in 2009 as a novelty act, parodying popular hits and remixing songs in a [[Country rap|hick-hop]] style, then in 2013 they went "serious" with their debut ''Tiller Gang'' release.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/redneck-souljers-mn0003255572/biography|title=Redneck Souljers {{!}} Biography & History {{!}} AllMusic|last=Jeffries|first=David|website=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=October 3, 2018}}</ref> Their first full-length studio album, ''Firewater'', debuted on the [[Billboard charts|''Billboard'' charts]].
