'''Jordan James McIntosh''' (born December 20, 1995<ref>{{cite web|title=Buzz: Jordan McIntosh|url=http://www.family.ca/buzz/jordan-mcintosh/|website=[[Family Channel (Canadian TV network)|Family Channel]]|accessdate=June 3, 2015}}{{dead link|date=November 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>) is a Canadian [[Country music|country]] singer-songwriter from [[Ottawa]], Ontario.<ref name="valley">{{cite web|url=http://www.insideottawavalley.com/news-story/3869005-jordan-mcintosh-is-living-the-dream/|title=Jordan Mcintosh is living the dream|first=Tara|last=Gesner|date=June 27, 2013|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmt.ca/artist/jordan-mcintosh/|title=CMT.ca: Jordan McIntosh biography|publisher=}}</ref> He has released a number of singles.

==Career==
He was born to Greg and Julie McIntosh in [[Ottawa]], Ontario.<ref name="valley" /> He has an older sister Melissa.<ref name="valley" /> He is presently based in [[Carleton Place]].<ref name="valley" />

McIntosh's first single, "Walk Away", was released in August 2012, followed by "Let Me Love You", "Grew Up in a Country Song", "That Girl" and "[[Story of My Life (One Direction song)|Story of My Life]]" which featured [[George Canyon]]. He also was featured on Canyon's Christmas song "Home for Christmas", which also included [[Aaron Pritchett]] and [[One More Girl]].

McIntosh won Ottawa Idol (formerly called Kiwanis Idol) in 2011. McIntosh won the 2014 Country Music Association of Ontario award for Rising Star.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Gesner|first1=Tara|title=Country heartthrob Jordan McIntosh named CMAO's Rising Star|url=http://www.insideottawavalley.com/news-story/4573866-country-heartthrob-jordan-mcintosh-named-cmao-s-rising-star/|accessdate=June 3, 2015|work=Inside Ottawa Valley|date=June 12, 2014}}</ref> He was a finalist in the Emerging Artist Showcase presented by [[Sirius XM Canada]] at the [[Boots and Hearts Music Festival]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Jordan McIntosh to appear in emerging artist showcase at Boots and Hearts Music Festival|url=http://www.insideottawavalley.com/whatson-story/3898453-jordan-mcintosh-to-appear-in-emerging-artist-showcase-at-boots-and-hearts-music-festival/|accessdate=June 3, 2015|work=Inside Ottawa Valley|date=July 18, 2013}}</ref>
