'''Chaley Rose''' is an American actress and singer. She is best known for her role as Zoey in the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] drama series ''[[Nashville (2012 TV series)|Nashville]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tvline.com/2013/07/23/nashville-season-2-cast-chaley-rose-scarletts-friend/ |title=Nashville Casts Newcomer Chaley Rose as Scarlett's Childhood Best Friend|publisher=tvline.com|date=July 23, 2013}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Chaley Rose Jackson was born on November 29, 1986 and raised in the city of [[Columbus, Indiana]]. She attended Central Middle School and Columbus East High School where she was a cheerleader, gymnast, and on the track team. She was the school's first Black homecoming queen. After high school, she graduated from Indiana University. She then spent time studying acting in [[New York City]] before moving to [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://swirlgirlarmy.com/post/63474125915/meet-swirl-girl-icon-chaley-rose/ |title=Meet Swirl Girl Icon Chaley Rose|publisher=swirlgirlarmy.com}}</ref>  Chaley Rose's parents are Cheryl Owsley Jackson, a reporter, and Charles (Chuck) Jackson, an artist. They were married for more than 20 years, now divorced.{{citation needed|date=March 2015}} She has one sibling, a brother, Charles E. Jackson, Jr. (Chase).
