'''Kelsey Hart''' is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[Kentucky]] signed to [[Curb Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.opry.com/artists/kelsey-hart|title=Kelsey Hart: Opry|website=Grand Ole Opry|accessdate=May 5, 2024}}</ref>

==Background==
After several years of songwriting for other artists under Word Music Publishing, Hart signed his own record deal with [[Curb Records]] in January 2023.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2023/01/kelsey-hart-signs-with-curb-records/|title=Kelsey Hart Signs With Curb Records|author=Lorie Hollabaugh|date=January 23, 2023|website=Music Row|accessdate=May 5, 2024}}</ref> His debut single, "Life with You", was released in November 2023, and charted on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Country Airplay]] chart. It served as the lead single and title track to his debut studio album, which was released on May 31, 2024.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.curb.com/2024/05/curb-records-artist-kelsey-hart-builds-autobiographical-debut-around-life-with-you-slated-to-drop-may-31-2024/|title=Curb Records Artist Kelsey Hart Builds Autobiographical Debut Around Life With You, Slated To Drop May 31, 2024|date=May 3, 2024|website=Curb Records|accessdate=May 5, 2024}}</ref>

Hart made his [[Grand Ole Opry]] debut on June 28, 2024.<ref name="Life with You Album"/>
