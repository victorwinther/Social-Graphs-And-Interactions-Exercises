'''Austin Webb''' (born in [[Williamston, South Carolina]]<ref>{{cite news|last=Bjorke |first=Matt |title=Music Video: Austin Webb – It's All Good |url=http://www.roughstock.com/video/music-video-austin-webb-it-s-all-good |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130411180635/http://www.roughstock.com/video/music-video-austin-webb-it-s-all-good |url-status=dead |archive-date=April 11, 2013 |accessdate=March 25, 2013 |newspaper=Roughstock |date=March 13, 2013 }}</ref>) is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter. Webb moved to [[Nashville, Tennessee]] in 2011 after winning a songwriting competition in Greenville.<ref>{{cite news|title=Local singer Austin Webb making music in Nashville|url=http://thejournalonline.com/2012/11/20/local-singer-austin-webb-making-music-in-nashville/|accessdate=March 25, 2013|newspaper=The Williamston Journal|date=November 20, 2012}}</ref>

In 2012, Webb signed with [[Streamsound Records]], a label founded by [[Byron Gallimore]] and [[Jim Wilkes]].<ref name="musicrow">{{cite news|last=Freeman|first=Jon|title=Streamsound Records Signs New Artist|url=http://www.musicrow.com/2012/10/streamsound-records-signs-new-artist/|accessdate=March 25, 2013|newspaper=[[MusicRow]]|date=October 11, 2012}}</ref> He released his debut single, "It's All Good", in December 2012.<ref name="musicrow" /> The song debuted at number 53 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Country Airplay]] chart in February 2013.<ref>{{cite news|last=Morris|first=Edward|title=Gary Allan Wears Triple ''Billboard'' Crowns This Week|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1701230/gary-allan-wears-triple-billboard-crowns-this-week.jhtml|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130205045211/http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1701230/gary-allan-wears-triple-billboard-crowns-this-week.jhtml|url-status=dead|archive-date=February 5, 2013|accessdate=March 25, 2013|newspaper=[[Country Music Television]]|date=February 2, 2013}}</ref> It will be included on an album produced by Gallimore.<ref>{{cite news|last=Dauphin|first=Chuck|title=615 Spotlight: Austin Webb Shops 'Good' Single as He Preps Debut Album|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-615/1536351/615-spotlight-austin-webb-shops-good-single-as-he-preps-debut-album|accessdate=March 25, 2013|newspaper=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=January 30, 2013}}</ref> Webb released his second single, "Slip On By", in July 2013 and it peaked on the ''Billboard'' Country Airplay chart at number 40 in early 2014.<ref>{{Cite magazine |url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5694851/austin-webb/chart |title=Austin Webb - Chart history &#124; Billboard |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |access-date=April 17, 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151212140024/http://www.billboard.com/artist/5694851/austin-webb/chart |archive-date=December 12, 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Webb made his debut on the [[Grand Ole Opry]] on November 9, 2013.<ref>[https://www.goupstate.com/article/NC/20131114/ENT/311141005/SJ/1097?p=1&tc=pg] {{dead link|date=March 2020}}</ref>

In December 2013, Webb made his debut national anthem performance at the [[Green Bay Packers]] [[Lambeau Field]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://wncy.com/blogs/sports/711/photos-go-behind-the-scenes-when-a-recording-artist-sings-the-national-anthem-at-lambeau/|title=PHOTOS :: Go Behind the Scenes When a Recording Artist Sings the National Anthem at Lambeau|website=Wncy.com}}</ref>

Early in 2014 Webb was selected to appear on the first leg of "The Country Deep Tour" presented by [[AT&T]] [[U-Verse]] and featuring [[Jana Kramer]] and [[Canaan Smith]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://countrydeeptour.att.net/ |title=Home &#124; the Country Deep™ Tour |accessdate=April 17, 2014 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140419014051/http://countrydeeptour.att.net/ |archive-date=April 19, 2014 }}</ref>

In early March 2014, Webb's third single "Raise 'Em Up" was released and debuted at number 58 on the ''Billboard'' Country Airplay chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.countryschatter.com/2014/03/austin-webb-releases-new-single-raise-em-up/|title=Austin Webb releases new single, Raise 'Em Up|website=Countryschatter.com|date=March 29, 2014}}</ref>

In January 2015, Webbs fourth single "All Country On You" was released. The song peaked at number 38 on ''Billboard'' Country Airplay Chart.
