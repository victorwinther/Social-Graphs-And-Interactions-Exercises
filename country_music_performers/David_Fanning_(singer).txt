'''David Fanning''' is an American [[country music]] singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is one-fourth of the production team [[New Voice Entertainment]], which has produced for [[Parmalee]] and [[Thompson Square]] and serves as [[Jason Aldean]]'s road band.<ref name="boot"/>

David Fanning has produced multiple hits (including 10 top 40s and 2 #1s) for the Thompson Square and Parmalee. These hits include three #1's ("[[Are You Gonna Kiss Me or Not]]", "[[If I Didn't Have You (Thompson Square song)|If I Didn't Have You]]", and "[[Carolina (Parmalee song)|Carolina]]").

In 2014, Fanning signed to Red Bow Records, a division of [[Broken Bow Records]]. He released his solo debut single "[[Drink You Away]]", a cover of a [[Justin Timberlake]] song.<ref name="redbowrecords2">{{cite web|url=http://redbowrecords.com/news/david-fannings-drink-you-away-studio-cut-music-video-world-premieres-on-siriusxm-blog-today/|title=DAVID FANNING'S "DRINK YOU AWAY" (STUDIO CUT) MUSIC VIDEO WORLD PREMIERES ON THE SiriusXM BLOG TODAY! &#124; Red Bow Records|publisher=redbowrecords.com|accessdate=2014-07-05|archive-date=2014-05-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140525233029/http://redbowrecords.com/news/david-fannings-drink-you-away-studio-cut-music-video-world-premieres-on-siriusxm-blog-today/|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="cmt">{{cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1726887/david-fanning-gets-support-from-justin-timberlake-and-jason-aldean.jhtml|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140525213424/http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1726887/david-fanning-gets-support-from-justin-timberlake-and-jason-aldean.jhtml|url-status=dead|archive-date=May 25, 2014|title=CMT : News : David Fanning Gets Support From Justin Timberlake and Jason Aldean|publisher=[[CMT (American TV channel)|CMT]]|access-date=2014-05-25}}</ref> Markos Papadatos of Digital Journal gave the song 4.5 stars out of 5.<ref name="digitaljournal">{{cite web|url=http://digitaljournal.com/entertainment/review-country-singer-david-fanning-rocks-on-new-single-drink-you-away/article/371352|title=Review: Country singer David Fanning rocks on new single 'Drink You Away' (Includes first-hand account)|publisher=digitaljournal.com|accessdate=2014-07-05}}</ref> The song debuted at number 60 on the [[Country Airplay]] chart dated for the week ending May 24, 2014, and peaked at number 58. Two more singles followed in 2015, "Doin' Country Right" and "What's Next," before Fanning parted ways with Red Bow in 2016.

Fanning released his debut EP ''First'' on September 23, 2016 via MV2 Entertainment.
