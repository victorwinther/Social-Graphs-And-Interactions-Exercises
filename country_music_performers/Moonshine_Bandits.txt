'''Moonshine Bandits''' is an American [[country rap]] group composed of Dusty "Tex" Dahlgren and Brett "Bird" Brooks.<ref name="allmusic">{{cite web|last=Jeffries|first=David|title=Moonshine Bandits {{!}} Biography|url={{Allmusic|class=artist|id=mn0000928079/biography|pure_url=yes}}|work=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=September 4, 2014}}</ref> The duo formed in [[California]] in 2003 and has released a number of albums, the most recent being ''The Whiskey Never Dries'' released in August 2019.

==Music career==
The duo was formed in [[California]] in 2003 and released their debut album, ''Soggy Crackerz''.<ref name="allmusic" /> They met up with Ty Weathers who is the founder of Burn County Music, which has continued to work closely in assisting majority of the Moonshine Bandits Blue Core/Dirt Rock sound. ''Soggy Crackerz'' was followed by ''Prohibition'' in 2006 and ''Divebars and Truckstops'' in 2010.<ref name="allmusic" />

Moonshine Bandits signed with [[Suburban Noize Records]] for the release of their fourth album, ''Whiskey and Women'', in 2011.<ref>{{cite news|title=Moonshine Bandits Sign A Record Deal|url=http://skopemag.com/2010/12/08/moonshine-bandits-sign-a-record-deal|accessdate=September 4, 2014|work=[[Skope Magazine]]|date=December 8, 2010}}</ref> The music video for "My Kind of Country" premiered on [[CMT Music|CMT Pure Country]] in February 2012 with a special appearance by Mike Allsup (The Bearded Guy playing the guitar) from [[Modesto, California]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Los Banos' Moonshine Bandits premier new video, 'My Kind of Country'|url=http://www.mercedsunstar.com/2012/02/21/2239373/los-banos-moonshine-bandits-premier.html|accessdate=September 4, 2014|work=[[Merced Sun-Star]]|date=February 21, 2012}}</ref> 

In 2014, they partnered with [[Average Joes Entertainment]] for their fifth album ''[[Calicountry]]''.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Conaway|first1=Alanna|title=The Moonshine Bandits to Release New Album Through Average Joes Entertainment|url=http://roughstock.com/news/the-moonshine-bandits-to-release-new-album-through-average-joes-entertainment/|accessdate=September 4, 2014|work=Roughstock|date=September 5, 2013|archive-url=https://archive.today/20140905004321/http://roughstock.com/news/the-moonshine-bandits-to-release-new-album-through-average-joes-entertainment/|archive-date=September 5, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> ''Calicountry'' sold 3,000 copies in its first week of release, debuting at number 22 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Top Country Albums]] chart and number 126 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bjorke|first1=Matt|title=Country Album Chart News: The Week of February 12, 2014: Florida Georgia Line #1, Eric Paslay Debuts Top 5; Whiskey Myers, Moonshine Bandits, Scotty McCreery|url=http://roughstock.com/news/country-album-chart-news-the-week-of-february-12-2014-florida-georgia-line-1-eric-paslay-debuts-top-5-whiskey-myers-moonshine-bandits-scotty-mccreery/|accessdate=September 4, 2014|work=Roughstock|date=February 12, 2014|archive-url=https://archive.today/20140905004322/http://roughstock.com/news/country-album-chart-news-the-week-of-february-12-2014-florida-georgia-line-1-eric-paslay-debuts-top-5-whiskey-myers-moonshine-bandits-scotty-mccreery/|archive-date=September 5, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>  

Their sixth album, ''[[Blacked Out]]'', was released in July 2015, and it debuted at No.158 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] and No. 13 on the Top Country Albums chart, No. 10 on the Top Rap Albums chart, selling 4,000 copies in its debut week.<ref>{{cite news|title=Country Album Chart Report for July 28, 2015|url=http://roughstock.com/news/2015/07/36603-country-album-chart-report-july-28-2015 |last= Bjorke|first=Matt |publisher=Roughstock |date= July 28, 2015 }} Sales figure given [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1_bRZs8MP6YBv3ww5nYxeGefsT_tgpVFDy4k5J-EAGRw/edit#gid=0 here]</ref>
