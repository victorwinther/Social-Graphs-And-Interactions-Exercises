'''Haley & Michaels''' is an American [[country music]] duo composed of Shannon Haley and Ryan Michaels, both born in [[California]].<ref name="billboard">{{cite magazine|last1=Dauphin|first1=Chuck|title=Haley & Michaels Ready to Break Out With 'One More Night to Break'|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/6281841/shannon-haley-ryan-michaels-interview|accessdate=June 3, 2015|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=October 15, 2014}}</ref> They released an extended play, ''Haley & Michaels'', independently on October 21, 2014, and a full album ''Hail Mary'' on November 15, 2019 under the label [[Hickory Records]].

==Career==
Shannon Haley and Ryan Michaels met in January 2011, introduced by friends at a coffee shop in [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Murtagh|first1=Heather|title=Nashville duo brings show to Redwood City|url=http://archives.smdailyjournal.com/article_preview.php?id=1756427|accessdate=June 3, 2015|work=[[San Mateo Daily Journal]]|date=October 15, 2012}}</ref> They married on May 9, 2015 in [[Saratoga, California]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Conaway|first1=Alanna|title=EXCLUSIVE: Rising Duo Haley & Michaels Marry in Northern California|url=http://www.countryweekly.com/news/exclusive-rising-duo-haley-michaels-marry-northern-california|accessdate=June 3, 2015|work=[[Country Weekly]]|date=May 11, 2015}}</ref> In March 2020, they announced expecting their first child a daughter in early October.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://people.com/parents/haley-and-michaels-pregnant-expecting-first-child-daughter-exclusive/|title=Country Duo Haley & Michaels Reveal She's Pregnant with a Baby Girl: 'It's a Gift to Have This Now'|website=PEOPLE.com|language=en|access-date=2020-03-28}}</ref>

They released their debut single, "Just Another Love Song", in April 2014.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Thompson|first1=Gayle|title=New Country Duo Haley & Michaels Release Debut Single|url=http://theboot.com/haley-michaels-just-another-love-song/|accessdate=June 3, 2015|work=The Boot|date=April 27, 2014}}</ref> They co-wrote the song with [[Richie McDonald]] of [[Lonestar]]; the song itself references that band's 1999 hit "[[Amazed]]", and features McDonald singing that song's chorus.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/haley-michaels-richie-mcdonald-just-another-love-song-story-behind-song/|title=Haley & Michaels, Lonestar's Richie McDonald Share Story Behind 'Just Another Love Song' – Exclusive Video Read More: Haley & Michaels Share Story Behind 'Just Another Love Song'|last=Dukes|first=Billy|date=8 May 2015|work=[[Taste of Country]]|accessdate=4 June 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Whitaker|first1=Sterling|title=Haley & Michaels, 'Just Another Love Song' Lyric Video – Exclusive Premiere|url=http://theboot.com/haley-michaels-just-another-love-song-lyric-video/|accessdate=June 3, 2015|work=The Boot|date=June 16, 2014}}</ref> It peaked at number 59 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Country Airplay]] chart in January 2015.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/biz/search/charts?f%5B0%5D=ts_chart_artistname%3AHaley%20%26%20Michaels&f%5B1%5D=ss_bb_type%3Achart_item&type=2&artist=Haley%20%26%20Michaels|title=Haley & Michaels Album & Song Chart History|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|publisher=[[Prometheus Global Media]]|accessdate=June 3, 2015}}</ref>

In January 2018, the duo were signed to [[Hickory Records]].  Their first single released under the label was "All Out".<ref>{{cite magazine |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/country/8094791/haley-michaels-all-out-hickory |title=Haley & Michaels Share New Single 'All Out,' Marking Hickory Records' Return to Country |date=January 18, 2018 |first= Chuck |last=Dauphin|magazine=Billboard }}</ref>

in 2019, their song "Hail Mary" was used as the anthem for the [[2019 San Francisco 49ers season|2019 season]] by the [[San Francisco 49ers]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.soundslikenashville.com/news/haley-michaels-hail-mary-49ers/ |title=Haley & Michaels Overcome Adversity With the Anthemic 'Hail Mary' | first= Kelly|last= Dearmore|date= September 9, 2019 |work=Sounds Like Nashville}}</ref> It was also featured in the [[Netflix]] film ''[[Walk. Ride. Rodeo.]]''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.thecountrynote.com/in-the-news/haley-michaels-hail-mary-featured-in-netflixs-walk-ride-rodeo/ |title= Haley & Michaels' "Hail Mary" Featured in Netflix's "Walk. Ride. Rodeo." |date=March 8, 2019 |work=The Country Note}}</ref> They released their first full album, ''Hail Mary'', in November 2019.{{cn|date=August 2023}}
