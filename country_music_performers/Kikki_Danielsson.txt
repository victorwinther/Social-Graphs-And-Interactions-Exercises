'''Ann-Kristin''' "'''Kikki'''" '''Danielsson''' (born 10 May 1952) is a Swedish [[country music|country]], [[dansband]] and pop singer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.discogs.com/artist/397366-Kikki-Danielsson|title=Kikki Danielsson|publisher=discogs|access-date=17 December 2014}}</ref> Sometimes, she also plays the [[accordion]] and she has also written some lyrics. She has gained notice for [[yodeling]] in some songs. Danielsson gained her largest popularity in the [[Nordic countries|Nordic region]] from the late 1970s until the late 1990s. She also gained popularity at the US country stage during the 1980s.<ref name="Poplight - Kikki Danielsson">{{Cite web |url=http://poplight.zitiz.se/artist/kikki-danielsson |title=Poplight – ''Kikki Danielsson'' |access-date=25 February 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101027144051/http://poplight.zitiz.se/artist/kikki-danielsson |archive-date=27 October 2010 |url-status=dead }}</ref> In 1986, she had the "Kikki i Nashville" TV show.

==Life and work==
Danielsson was born in [[Osby Municipality|Osby]], Sweden, and resides in [[Gävleborg]].

In total, she has participated nine times at the Swedish [[Melodifestivalen]] and one time at the Norwegian [[Melodi Grand Prix]]. She has participated in the [[Eurovision Song Contest]] twice, in [[Eurovision Song Contest 1982|1982]] (as a part of the Swedish pop and country group [[Chips (band)|Chips]] with the song "[[Dag efter dag]]", finishing 8th) and in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 1985|1985]] (solo) with the song "[[Bra vibrationer (song)|Bra vibrationer]]", finishing 3rd.

Kikki Danielsson was born in Osby, but was [[adoption|adopted]] and lived on a farm in [[Älmhult]], [[Småland]] from the age of five. There, she became the only child in the family while her younger sister was adopted to another place. As a five-year-old girl, Kikki Danielsson made her first singing performance, which occurred at the local [[Sunday school]]'s [[Saint Lucy's Day|Lucia]] party in her Småland home church, singing the Christmas song "När Jesusbarnet låg en gång". As a child, she sang in the local church choir.

As a 17-year-old girl, Kikki Danielsson begun as a singer in dansband Nickies and in 1973 she switched to another [[dansband]], [[Wizex]], with which she participated at the Swedish [[Melodifestivalen 1978]]. She left Wizex in 1982, but as a member of the pop and country group [[Chips (band)|Chips]], she participated at the Swedish [[Melodifestivalen 1980]], [[Melodifestivalen 1981|1981]] and [[Melodifestivalen 1982|1982]]. She has also been a popular [[Svensktoppen]] artist, with hits as the Melodifestivalen songs, but also other songs, like "[[Papaya Coconut (song)|Papaya Coconut]]" in 1987. She willingly sings country and has contributed in radio and TV shows in the US in the 1980s.

During the second half of 1984, she married Swedish musician [[Kjell Roos]], who led the dansband [[Roosarna]], with which she sang between 1990 and 1999. In 1985 she gave birth to their first child, a daughter. In late 1987 she released the [[Christmas music|Christmas]] album "[[Min barndoms jular]]". Together with Roosarna, she had many [[Svensktoppen]] hits during the 1990s. In 1994, the "dansband of the year" prize at the [[Grammis]] was awarded to Kikki Danielsson & Roosarna. In 1999 she separated from Kjell Roos. However, the musical co-operation restarted in 2003.<ref>[http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesliv/musik/article122891.ab Aftonbladet 18 February 2003 – ''Kikki sjunger ihop med sin ex-man igen'']</ref>

Kikki Danielsson has also sung at the 2001 [[Stockholm Pride]]. Together with [[Elisabeth Andreassen]] and [[Lotta Engberg]] she participated at the Swedish [[Melodifestivalen 2002]] and together they called themselves [[Kikki, Bettan & Lotta]] and had an own [[dinner show]] during the second half of 2002 and participated at the Norwegian Melodi Grand Prix in 2003. They also started a successful collaboration with the Swedish brand "twilfit" designing stretchy yet fashionable T-shirts.

Kikki Danielsson participated at [[Melodifestivalen 2006]], which is the Swedish qualifications for the [[Eurovision Song Contest]]. She performed with the song "[[I dag & i morgon (song)|I dag & i morgon]]", written by [[Thomas G:son]] and Calle Kindbom, which reached the final in the [[Stockholm Globe Arena]], where it finished at the 10th and last place. Totally, she has participated nine times at the Swedish Melodifestivalen and one time at Norwegian Melodi Grand Prix. In 2018, she participated in [[Melodifestivalen]] once again.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/melodifestivalen/a/l11943/har-ar-deltagarna-i-melodifestivalen-2018|title=Här är deltagarna i Melodifestivalen 2018|work=Aftonbladet|language=sv|author=Torbjörn Ek|date=28 November 2017|access-date=20 January 2018}}</ref>

From 28 November to 17 December 2006, she toured Sweden with a band on the "Julstämning med Kikki" Christmas concert tour. Her periods of [[alcoholism|problems with alcohol]] have been a popular topic of the Scandinavian [[News media|press]]<ref>[http://www.vg.no/musikk/artikkel.php?artid=211624 Artisten Kikki Danielsson (51) er flau, fortvilet og lei seg etter at hun fredag ble tatt for fyllekjøring for annen gang.]</ref><ref>[http://www.vg.no/musikk/artikkel.php?artid=212170 Kikki Danielsson har lagt seg inn på behandlingshjem for å få profesjonell hjelp for sine alkoholproblemer.]</ref><ref>[http://www.vg.no/musikk/artikkel.php?artid=215149 Fyllekjøringsdømte Kikki Danielsson velger avvenning framfor turné – og får Elisabeth Andreassens fulle støtte.]</ref> (Danielsson first started drinking heavily to self-medicate as a result of her [[rheumatoid arthritis]]),<ref>[https://www.aftonbladet.se/noje/0006/04/kikki.html ’Jag drack för att stå ut’ – Kikki om kampen mot sina alkoholproblem]</ref> as has her [[overweight|weight problem]], in which she has become obese.<ref>[http://gfx.aftonbladet-cdn.se/multimedia/archive/00219/NOJE-27s26-faks-42__219358w.jpg Aftonbladet cover: Kikki Danielssons svåra kamp mot fetman "Kikki Danielsson's hard fight against obesity"]{{Dead link|date=December 2022 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>[http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/article238912.ab Jag tycker att jag är ful – Kikki om sin fetma: Jag tröståt falukorv]</ref> Because of her weight problem, she appeared in ''Du är vad du äter'', the Swedish version of "[[You Are What You Eat]]", in 2006 where she made great progress and lost several pounds.<ref>[http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/article399033.ab?service=print Kikki rasar i vikt – Superdieten har fått stjärnan att slopa kanelbullar och korv]</ref>

In January 2010, it was announced that Kikki Danielsson together with [[Sören Karlsson|Sören "Sulo" Karlsson]] and [[The Diamond Dogs]] would record the single ''Maybe I'll Do'',<ref>[http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/article6466006.ab Aftonbladet 21 January 2010 – ''Kikki Danielssons nya rockkarriär'']</ref> and on 13 July 2010 she appeared at [[Allsång på Skansen]]. On 13 April 2011 she released the album ''[[Första dagen på resten av mitt liv]]'', where she worked together with Sören "Sulo" Karlsson.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ginza.se/Product/Product.aspx?Identifier=106606 |title=Ginza Musik |access-date=13 April 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110426060250/http://www.ginza.se/Product/Product.aspx?Identifier=106606 |archive-date=26 April 2011 }}</ref>

In 2012, Danielsson starred in the film ''[[Sean Banan inuti Seanfrika|Sean Banan inuti Seanafrika]]''.

In 2017, she participated in the eighth season of ''[[Så mycket bättre]]'' ([[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]]).<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nsk.se/2017/03/20/kikki-danielsson-klar-for-sa-mycket-battre-2/|title=Kikki Danielsson klar för Så mycket bättre|publisher=Norra Skåne|language=sv|author=Pernilla Ekdahl|date=20 March 2017|access-date=17 December 2017|archive-date=22 December 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171222051612/http://www.nsk.se/2017/03/20/kikki-danielsson-klar-for-sa-mycket-battre-2/|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Kikki Danielsson performed again at [[Melodifestivalen 2018|Melodifestivalen]] on 3 February 2018, this time with the song "Osby Tennessee",<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://eurovision.tv/story/2018-sweden-melodifestivalen-acts-revealed|title=Artists participating in Sweden's Melodifestivalen 2018 are revealed!|access-date=28 January 2018}}</ref> which ended up seventh and last in the semi-final following votings.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.sn.se/kultur-noje/sulo-och-kikki-nojda-trots-jumboplats/|title=Sulo och Kikki nöjda|publisher=Södermanlands nyheter|language=sv|author=Tobias Johannesson, Lina Nydahl|date=3 February 2018|access-date=2 April 2018}}</ref>

In February 2020, Kikki Danielsson was elected into the Melodifestivalen Hall of Fame.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://blogg.svt.se/melodifestivalen-expertbloggen/kikki-danielsson-valjs-in-in-melodifestivalens-hall-of-fame/|title=Kikki Danielsson väljs in i Melodifestivalens Hall of Fame|newspaper=Sveriges Television|language=sv|author=Gustav Dahlander|date=5 February 2020|access-date=8 March 2020}}</ref>
