'''Amy Metcalfe''' (born March 2, 1992) is a Canadian [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[Red Deer, Alberta]] who signed to [[Royalty Records]] in 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|title = AMY METCALFE SIGNED TO ROYALTY RECORDS! {{!}} Royalty Records|url = http://royaltyrecords.ca/?p=5357|website = Royalty Records| date=July 3, 2014 |accessdate = October 13, 2015|language = en-us}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://amymetcalfe.com|title=Amy Metcalfe - Official Website|work=Amy Metcalfe}}</ref>

On April 27, 2015, Metcalfe released her debut EP, ''Inside Out'', on [[Royalty Records]] with distribution from [[Sony Music Canada|Sony Entertainment Canada]]. On June 22, she followed the EP by releasing a new single called "Whiskey Won't Fix Me" which is a duet with [[New Brunswick]] native [[Tristan Horncastle]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = "Whiskey Won't Fix Me", new single and VIDEO by Amy Metcalfe (Featuring Tristan Horncastle)|url = http://www.byronhillmusic.com/whiskey-wont-fix-me-a-single-by-amy-metcalfe-featuring-tristan-horncastle/|website = www.byronhillmusic.com|accessdate = October 13, 2015|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181010095920/https://www.byronhillmusic.com/whiskey-wont-fix-me-a-single-by-amy-metcalfe-featuring-tristan-horncastle/|archive-date = October 10, 2018|url-status = dead}}</ref>

In 2017 Amy wrote and released a pop/edm song with Calgary DJ “Dj Kav” under the name AEM. The song was debuted in an opening slot with [[The Chainsmokers]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=DJ Kav Letting Go - Google Search|url=https://www.google.ca/search?kgmid=/g/11dyml81xd&hl=en-CA&q=dj+kav+letting+go&kgs=f6f7a0321086e6f0&shndl=0&source=sh/x/kp/osrp&entrypoint=sh/x/kp/osrp|access-date=2020-11-12|website=www.google.ca}}</ref>
