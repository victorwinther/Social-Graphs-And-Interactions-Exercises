'''Greg Bates''' (born September 25, 1987) is an American [[country music]] singer formerly signed to [[Republic Nashville]]. Bates also is a songwriter, producer, singer, and guitarist.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Greg Bates |url=https://www.songfinch.com/artists/82855 |access-date=2023-05-10 |website=Songfinch |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-06-12 |title=Ten Years After "Did It for the Girl," Greg Bates is Right Where He Wants to Be |url=https://procountrymusic.com/2022/06/11/ten-years-after-did-it-for-the-girl-greg-bates-is-right-where-he-wants-to-be/ |access-date=2023-05-10 |website=Pro Country |language=en}}</ref>

==Early life==
In 2006, Bates graduated from [[Ravenwood High School]]. Bates then graduated in 2010 with a degree in Music Business from [[Belmont University]].<ref name="usatoday"/> He signed a recording contract in the fall of 2011.<ref name="usatoday">[https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/music/2012/11/18/did-it-for-the-girl-greg-bates-on-the-verge/1710439/ On the Verge: Country singer Greg Bates] USA Today. Retrieved on November 3, 2012.</ref> Bates quietly parted ways with Republic Nashville towards the end of 2013.

Stanton is Shelley's hometown. The two posted up wedding pictures on Instagram.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://theboot.com/greg-bates-married/ |title=Greg Bates Gets Married |website=Theboot.com |date= May 7, 2015|accessdate=March 21, 2020}}</ref>

==Musical career==
In April 2012, Bates released his debut single "Did It for the Girl", which debuted at Number 57 on the [[Hot Country Songs]] chart dated for April 28, 2012.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20120423033736/http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1683569/lionel-richies-tuskegee-recaptures-no-1.jhtml News : Lionel Richie's Tuskegee Recaptures No. 1]. CMT (April 21, 2012). Retrieved on November 13, 2012.</ref> Billy Dukes gave the song four stars out of five, saying that it is "instantly memorable — if only for its clean simplicity."<ref>[http://tasteofcountry.com/greg-bates-did-it-for-the-girl/ Greg Bates, ‘Did It for the Girl’ – Song Review]. Tasteofcountry.com (April 20, 2012). Retrieved on November 13, 2012.</ref> Bates made his [[Grand Ole Opry]] debut in April 2012.<ref>[http://tasteofcountry.com/greg-bates-grand-ole-opry/ Greg Bates Makes His Debut at the Grand Ole Opry]. Tasteofcountry.com (May 3, 2012). Retrieved on November 13, 2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.musicrow.com/2012/05/greg-bates-makes-opry-debut/ Greg Bates Makes Opry Debut : MusicRow – Nashville's Music Industry Publication – News, Songs From Music City]. Musicrow.com (May 7, 2012). Retrieved on November 13, 2012.</ref> The album's second single, "Fill in the Blank," was released to country radio on February 11, 2013. Bates has received air play as a "Highway Find" on satellite radio channel [[The Highway (Sirius XM)]].
