'''Elise Davis''' is an American [[country music|country]] musician<ref name="myspace">{{cite web|last1=Tan|first1=Emily|title=Elise Davis on 'The Token': "It's Never Been Straight-Up Country Music"|url=https://myspace.com/article/2017/2/7/elise-davis-token-interview|publisher=[[Myspace]]|accessdate=9 February 2017}}</ref> from [[Little Rock, Arkansas]].

==Career==
Elise Davis began her career in 2016, releasing her first full-length album titled ''The Token''.<ref name="noiseyvice">{{cite web|last1=Domenighini|first1=Annalise|title=Elise Davis's Debut Country Album 'The Token' Puts Gender and Love Toe-to-Toe|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/elise-davis-debut-album-the-token-premiere|publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|accessdate=9 February 2017}}</ref><ref name="rollingstone">{{cite magazine|last1=Menconi|first1=David|title=Hear Elise Davis Celebrate Hooking Up in Unrepentant 'Penny'|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/country/premieres/hear-elise-davis-celebrate-hooking-up-in-unrepentant-penny-w435979|magazine=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=24 August 2016 |accessdate=9 February 2017}}</ref><ref name="americansongwriter">{{cite web|last1=O'Rear|first1=Caine|title=Song Premiere: Elise Davis, "I Just Want Your Love"|date=5 August 2016 |url=https://americansongwriter.com/2016/08/song-premiere-elise-davis-just-want-love/|publisher=[[American Songwriter]]|accessdate=9 February 2017}}</ref><ref name="elmoremagazine">{{cite web|last1=Berick|first1=Michael|title=Elise Davis: The Token|url=http://www.elmoremagazine.com/2016/11/reviews/albums/elise-davis|work=[[Elmore Magazine]]|date=29 November 2016 |accessdate=9 February 2017}}</ref><ref name="popmatters">{{cite web|last1=Rivitz|first1=Will|title=Elise Davis - "Finally" (audio) (premiere)|url=http://www.popmatters.com/post/elise-davis-finally-audio-premiere/|publisher=[[PopMatters]]|accessdate=9 February 2017}}</ref> Davis released her second full-length album, ''Cactus'', in September 2018.<ref name="brooklynvegan">{{cite web |last1=Sacher |first1=Andrew |title=Elise Davis releasing 'Cactus' (watch the "Lone Wolf" video) |url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/elise-davis-releasing-cactus-watch-the-lone-wolf-video/ |publisher=[[BrooklynVegan]] |accessdate=22 January 2019}}</ref>

In January 2021, Davis announced her third album titled ''Anxious. Happy. Chill.'', set for an April 16 release via Tone Tree Music.<ref>{{Cite news |date=January 22, 2021 |title=Elise Davis Announces New Album ''Anxious. Happy. Chill.'' Out 4/16 |work=Glide Magazine |url=https://glidemagazine.com/252710/elise-davis-announces-new-album-anxious-happy-chill-out-4-16/ |access-date=April 2, 2021}}</ref>
