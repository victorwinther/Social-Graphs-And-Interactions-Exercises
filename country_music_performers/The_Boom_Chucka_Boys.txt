'''The Boom Chucka Boys''' is a Canadian [[country music|country]] and [[rockabilly]] band<ref name="express"/> from [[Sylvan Lake, Alberta]] composed of Ryan Langlois, Joel Rathjen, Teddy Roy Michaylow, Ben Shillabeer and Neil MacDonald.<ref name="cbc">{{cite web|title=The Boom Chucka Boys on CBC Music|url=http://music.cbc.ca/#/artists/The-Boom-Chucka-Boys|website=[[CBC Music]]|accessdate=September 11, 2014}}</ref> The band was formed in July 2010.<ref name="cbc" /><ref name="express">{{cite news|last1=Weber|first1=Mark|title=Boom Chucka Boys unleash 'rockabilly and classic country'|url=http://www.reddeerexpress.com/entertainment/Boom_Chucka_Boys_unleash_rockabilly_and_classic_country_211255751.html|accessdate=September 11, 2014|work=Red Deer Express|date=June 12, 2013}}</ref> They were discovered by [[Gord Bamford]] in January 2011 and signed a record deal with his company, Cache Entertainment.<ref name="express" /><ref name="college">{{cite news|last1=Ledingham|first1=Lani|title=The Boom Chucka Boys will make you shake your boogie woogie|url=http://rdc.ab.ca/about/alumni-foundation/alumni/blog/boom-chucka-boys-will-make-you-shake-your-boogie-woogie|accessdate=September 11, 2014|work=[[Red Deer College]]|date=February 14, 2014}}</ref>

Their self-titled debut album was released in April 2013 and distributed by [[Sony Music Canada]].<ref name="college" /> It was produced by [[Byron Hill]] and recorded in [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref name="cbc" /> The first single, "Find My Peace of Mind", was released in March 2013 and peaked at number 43 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Canada Country]] chart.<ref name="Canada Country" /> In 2014, The Boom Chucka Boys were nominated for the [[Canadian Country Music Association]] Award for Group or Duo of the Year.<ref name="advocate">{{cite news|last1=Michelin|first1=Lana|title=Boom Chucka Boys have shot at country music group of year|url=http://www.reddeeradvocate.com/entertainment/Boom_Chucka_Boys_have_shot__at_country_music_group_of_year_268335022.html|accessdate=September 11, 2014|work=[[Red Deer Advocate]]|date=July 23, 2014}}</ref> The band signed with [[Royalty Records]] in 2014 and released their second album, ''Ramble'', on February 10, 2015.<ref name="advocate" /> The album features three singles: "Turn This Car Around" released in October 2014, "Rebel Wings" released in February 2015 and "Can't Take My Lips Off You" released in August 2015.
