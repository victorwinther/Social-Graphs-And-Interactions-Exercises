'''Owen Michael Riegling''' is a Canadian [[country music]] singer and songwriter. He is currently signed to [[Universal Music Canada]], and has charted with the singles "Love (The Sweater Song)", "[[Old Dirt Roads]]", and "Moonshines".<ref name="Umusic"/><ref name="Can Country"/>

==Early life==
Riegling grew up on a 100-acre farm in [[Mildmay, Ontario]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://cmaontario.ca/artist-of-the-month-owen-riegling-may-2022/|title=Artist of the Month: Owen Riegling|date=May 10, 2022|work=CMA Ontario|access-date=December 25, 2023}}</ref> He began playing the guitar at eleven years old, after receiving one as a Christmas gift.<ref name="FPM">{{cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/owen-riegling-interview/|title=Owen Riegling Talks About Music and New Single|date=September 15, 2019|work=Front Porch Music|access-date=December 25, 2023}}</ref> He performed for the first time as part of his guitar teacher's band (Country Indie Artist Tattoos 'N Whiskey), before progressing to play covers of [[Eric Church]] songs at local venues.<ref name="FPM"/> Riegling initially attended business school after high school, but realized he enjoyed playing music more. He elected to attend the [[Ontario Institute of Audio Recording Technology]] for music production, after he had won a free recording session in a contest.<ref name="FPM"/>

==Career==
In July 2019, Riegling independently released the song "Smoke Man".<ref name="FPM"/> The song was included on his debut extended play ''Empty Room'', which was released on September 4, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/owen-riegling-ep-empty-room/|title=Owen Riegling's Debut EP ''Empty Room'' Drops Today|date=September 4, 2020|work=Front Porch Music|access-date=December 25, 2023}}</ref> In August 2022, Riegling won the [[Boots and Hearts]] Emerging Artist Showcase contest, securing him a single release with [[Universal Music Canada]], songwriting sessions, and a trip to [[Nashville]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://theranch100.com/mildmays-owen-riegling-wins-emerging-artist-showcase-at-boots-hearts/|title=Mildmay's Owen Riegling wins Emerging Artist Showcase at Boots & Hearts|work=100.1 FM The Ranch|date=August 8, 2022|access-date=December 25, 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/owen-riegling-wins-boots-hearts-emerging-artist/|title=Owen Riegling Wins The 2022 Boots & Hearts Emerging Artist Showcase|date=August 7, 2022|work=Front Porch Music|access-date=December 25, 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.excal.on.ca/arts/2023/11/07/owen-riegling-small-town-country-music-star/|title=Owen Riegling: Small-town country music star|date=November 7, 2023|first=Kaila|last=Gallacher|work=Excalibur|access-date=December 25, 2023}}</ref>
