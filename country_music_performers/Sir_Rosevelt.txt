'''Sir Rosevelt''' was an American [[electronic dance music]] group consisting of [[Zac Brown]], [[Niko Moon]], and Ben Simonetti. The group, formed in 2016, has released one album for [[Elektra Records]].

==History==
Zac Brown, lead singer of [[Zac Brown Band]], first announced Sir Rosevelt in 2016. The group's other members are Niko Moon, a frequent co-writer of Brown's, and Ben Simonetti, who provided [[programming (music)|programming]] for several tracks on Zac Brown Band's ''[[Jekyll + Hyde]]'' album.<ref name="allmusic"/> Initially, Brown did not reveal the other members of the group, nor did he provide any press after the release of their first song "Sunday Finest".<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/country/premieres/hear-zac-browns-mysterious-side-project-sir-rosevelt-w441218|title=Hear Zac Brown's Mysterious Side Project Sir Rosevelt|last=Dunkerley|first=Beville|date=September 20, 2016|magazine=Rolling Stone|accessdate=14 March 2018}}</ref> Later in 2016, he announced the other members and released three more tracks.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7526468/zac-brown-sir-rosevelt-interview-dance-pop-edm-diplo-skrillex|title=Zac Brown Reveals Diplo & Skrillex-Influenced Dance-Pop Project Sir Rosevelt: Interview|last=Baltin|first=Steve|date=September 30, 2016|magazine=Billboard|accessdate=March 14, 2018}}</ref> An album came in December 2017, with the single "Somethin' 'Bout You".<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8031079/zac-brown-sir-rosevelt-album-single-something-bout-you-timbaland|title=Zac Brown Announces Sir Rosevelt Album & New Song 'Something 'Bout You'|last=Armstrong|first=Megan|date=November 10, 2017|magazine=Billboard|accessdate=March 14, 2018}}</ref> Also preceding the album's release was another teaser track titled "The Bravest" which was used by the German television channel ZDF for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nashcountrydaily.com/2017/11/17/zac-browns-new-trio-sir-rosevelt-releases-new-song-the-bravest-listen/|title=Zac Brown's New Trio, Sir Rosevelt, Releases New Song, "The Bravest" [Listen]|last=Casey|first=Jim|date=November 17, 2017|work=Nash Country Daily|accessdate=March 14, 2018}}</ref>
