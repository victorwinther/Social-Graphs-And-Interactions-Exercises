'''William Michael Morgan''' (born May 13, 1993) is an American [[country music]] singer from [[Vicksburg, Mississippi]]. He was signed to [[Warner Bros. Records|Warner Bros. Nashville]].<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.warnermusicnashville.com/william-michael-morgan |title=Warner Music Nashville William Michael Morgan |publisher=Warnermusicnashville.com |date= |accessdate=2015-11-17}}</ref>

His debut single "[[I Met a Girl (William Michael Morgan song)|I Met a Girl]]", reached number two on the [[Country Airplay]] chart and number eight on the [[Hot Country Songs]] chart in October 2016; the song was co-written with [[Sam Hunt]].<ref>{{cite web|author=|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/william-michael-morgan-i-met-a-girl/ |title=ToC Critic's Pick: William Michael Morgan, 'I Met a Girl' |publisher=Tasteofcountry.com |date= 19 August 2015|accessdate=2015-11-17}}</ref> The album's second single "Missing", was released to country radio on November 7, 2016. His debut studio album ''[[Vinyl (William Michael Morgan album)|Vinyl]]'', was released on September 30, 2016. Morgan parted ways with Warner Bros. Nashville in January 2019.
