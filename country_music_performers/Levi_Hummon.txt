'''Levi Hummon''' is an American [[country music|country]] musician. Hummon is the son of fellow country musician [[Marcus Hummon]].<ref name="theboot">{{cite web |last1=Thompson |first1=Gayle |title=Levi Hummon Sees His Parents' Influence on His Career |url=https://theboot.com/levi-hummon-parents-marcus-hummon-rev-becca-stevens/ |publisher=The Boot |accessdate=10 March 2019}}</ref><ref name="rollingstone">{{cite web |last1=Freeman |first1=Jon |title=Levi Hummon Stops Chasing Radio, Raises the Bar on New 'Patient' EP |date=21 December 2018 |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-country/levi-hummon-interview-patient-ep-771974/ |publisher=[[Rolling Stone]] |accessdate=10 March 2019}}</ref> Levi Hummon released his first self-titled EP in 2016.<ref name="musicrow">{{cite web |last1=Nicholson |first1=Jessica |title=Levi Hummon To Release Self-Titled EP April 1 |date=14 March 2016 |url=https://musicrow.com/2016/03/levi-hummon-to-release-self-titled-ep-april-1/ |publisher=[[MusicRow]] |accessdate=10 March 2019}}</ref><ref name="tasteofcountry">{{cite web |last1=Dukes |first1=Billy |title=Levi Hummon Answers His Country Calling |url=https://tasteofcountry.com/levi-hummon-interview-steven-tyler-ep-his-dad/ |publisher=Taste of Country |accessdate=10 March 2019}}</ref> In 2018, he released his second EP titled ''Patient''.<ref name="rollingstone"/> Hummon has opened for country music artists Tim McGraw, Keith Urban, and Lady Antebellum and performed multiple shows at the Grand Ole Opry. In 2019, he launched his first headlining tour, the ''Drop of Us Tour'' with 16 tour dates across the United States. In 2019, he released his single, "State I'm In." 

In 2019, Hummon was named the face of Amazon Music's official ''Introducing: Country'' playlist along with the cover of Spotifyâ€™s ''Hot Country'' playlist. He opened for [[Hunter Hayes]] on the [[Closer to You Tour]].{{cn|date=November 2022}}
