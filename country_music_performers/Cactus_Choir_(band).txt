'''Cactus Choir''' was an American [[country music]] group from [[San Francisco|San Francisco, California]], composed of Marty Atkinson (vocals), Gary Hooker (guitar), Dave Ristrim (steel guitar), Tim Hensley (banjo), Shane Hicks (keyboards), Cal Ball (bass) and Eric Nelson (drums).<ref name="whitburn">{{cite book|last=Whitburn|first=Joel|title=Hot Country Songs 1944 to 2008|publisher=Record Research, Inc|page=74|year=2008|isbn=978-0-89820-177-2}}</ref> The group's self-titled debut album was released by [[Curb Records|Curb]]/[[Universal Records|Universal]] on March 24, 1998.<ref name="billboard">{{cite news|last=Price|first=Deborah Evans|title=Cactus Choir Thrives In The Studio|url=http://www.americanradiohistory.com/Archive-Billboard/90s/1998/BB-1998-02-07.pdf|accessdate=April 11, 2015|newspaper=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=February 7, 1998}}</ref> Jesse Mullins of ''American Cowboy'' gave the album a favorable review, writing that the musicians "come from varied musical backgrounds but have blended their talents well." Mullins compared their "wistful harmonies" to [[Restless Heart]] and [[Blackhawk (band)|Blackhawk]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Mullins|first1=Jesse|title=Reviews|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yuoCAAAAMBAJ&q=%22Cactus%20choir%22&pg=PA84|accessdate=April 11, 2015|work=American Cowboy|date=July–August 1998}}</ref>

The group's first single, "Step Right Up", was released on December 9, 1997.<ref>{{cite web|title=Step Right Up – Cactus Choir|url={{Allmusic|class=album|id=mw0001008106|pure_url=yes}}|work=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=April 11, 2015}}</ref> It peaked at number 62 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Country Songs|Hot Country Singles & Tracks]] chart.<ref name="whitburn" /> A music video for the song aired on [[CMT (U.S. TV channel)|CMT]].<ref name="billboard" />

In 2011, Atkinson formed the duo Blue Cactus Choir with Katy Boyd.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Puccl|first1=Mark|title=Marty Atkinson & Katy Boyd Join to Form Blue Cactus Choir|url=http://www.gratefulweb.com/articles/marty-atkinson-katy-boyd-join-form-blue-cactus-choir|accessdate=April 11, 2015|work=Grateful Web|date=June 12, 2012}}</ref>
