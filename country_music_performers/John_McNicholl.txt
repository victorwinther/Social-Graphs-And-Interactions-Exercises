'''John McNicholl''' is a country singer from [[Northern Ireland]].

McNicholl's first album, ''Something Old, Something New'' was released in 2004 and earned him the "Best Newcomer" award at the Irish World Awards.<ref>http://www.derryjournal.com/news/local/career-highs-for-singer-john-mcnicholl-i-wouldn-t-change-it-for-the-world-1-2120889 {{dead link|date=March 2020}}</ref> He also represented Ireland at the CMA Global Showcase in [[Nashville]]. Since then, he has gone on to record a number of albums each earning him a following in both Ireland and the United Kingdom.<ref>http://www.londonderrysentinel.co.uk/news/local/charity-barn-dance-with-top-country-and-western-star-john-mcnicholl-1-2102273 {{Dead link|date=February 2022}}</ref>
