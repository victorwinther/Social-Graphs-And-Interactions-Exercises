'''Konrad Wert''', known as '''Possessed by Paul James''' since 2005, is an American folk singer, songwriter and musician from [[Lee County, Florida]].<ref>[http://www.austinchronicle.com/music/2005-12-16/320177/ One in a Million]</ref>  His music is composed of string instruments including [[banjo]], [[guitar]] and [[fiddle]] with roots within [[Folk music|folk]], [[blues]] and [[Punk rock|punk]].  He was raised in a small [[Mennonite]] community among [[pacifists]], service workers and transplants. He lives in Texas with his wife and sons selling his music online and going on tours. He is also a special education teacher in Kerrville, Texas.<ref name="emu">{{cite web|url=http://emu.edu/now/news/2015/01/konrad-wert-a-k-a-possessed-by-paul-james-draws-spotlight-from-billboard-charts-npr-and-ny-times-before-returning-yet-again-to-emu/|title=Konrad Wert, a.k.a. Possessed by Paul James, draws spotlight from Billboard charts, NPR and NY Times before returning (yet again) to EMU|date=13 January 2015|accessdate=16 January 2015}}</ref> 

At the end of 2013 ''There Will Be Nights When I'm Lonely'' was released with Hillgrass Bluebilly Records. The album initially charted at #12 on [[Billboard Magazine]] under the Country/Bluegrass genre. [[National Public Radio]], [[Country Music Television|CMT]], [[MTV]] featured both articles and tracks from the release. ''[[The New York Times]]'' stated "PPJ [was] a one man folk wonder"<ref name="nytimes.com">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/slideshow/2014/08/05/arts/music/20140804PICKATHON.html#6|title = Photos from the Pickathon Music Festival|newspaper = The New York Times|date = 4 August 2014|last1 = Caramanica|first1 = Jon}}</ref> and was featured at one of the top concerts of the 2014 Pickathon Music Festival in Oregon.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2014/12/30/arts/music/best-concerts-of-2014-beyonc-lorde-st-vincent-romeo-santos-and-more.html?_r=0|title=Sounds That Lingered: Critics' Top 10 Concerts|newspaper=The New York Times|date=29 December 2014|last1=Pareles|first1=Jon}}</ref>
