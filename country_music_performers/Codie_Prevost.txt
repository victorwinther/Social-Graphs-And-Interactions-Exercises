'''Codie Prevost''' (born December 19, 1984) is a Canadian country music artist. He is a four time [[Canadian Country Music Association]] (CCMA) nominee and a six time Saskatchewan Country Music Association (SCMA) Entertainer of the Year and Male Vocalist of the Year winner.<ref>{{cite news|title='The Road Ahead' leads to Nipawin|url=http://eedition.nipawinjournal.com/doc/Nipawin-Journal/nipawinwww_072413/2013072301/7.html#6|accessdate=September 15, 2013|newspaper=The Nipawin Journal|date=July 24, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150614090414/http://eedition.nipawinjournal.com/doc/Nipawin-Journal/nipawinwww_072413/2013072301/7.html#6|archive-date=June 14, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> In total, he has received 26 SCMA Awards. Prevost's past performances include [[CMA Music Festival]], Dauphin Countryfest, [[South by Southwest]], American Tours Festival, [[Havelock Country Jamboree]], [[Craven Country Jamboree]] and Manitoulin Island Country Fest.

Prevost released two full-length albums and one EP on his own label, Good Spirit Records.<ref>{{cite news|last=Pescod|first=Nicolas|title=Rising country star Codie Prevost set for a busy year|url=http://www.canada.com/health/Rising+country+star+Codie+Prevost+busy+year/6418605/story.html|accessdate=September 15, 2013|newspaper=[[Postmedia News]]|date=April 5, 2012}}</ref> He started his climb onto the country music scene in 2005 after recording his debut album, ''The Road Ahead''. In 2010, he released his second album, ''Get Loud'', which helped him to earn the SCMA awards for Entertainer of the Year and Male Vocalist of the Year. Nashville Music Guide rated the album with 6 out of 7 stars.<ref>{{cite news|title=CD Review: Codie Prevost, "Get Loud"|url=http://www.nashvillemusicguide.com/cd-review-cody-prevost-get-loud/|accessdate=September 15, 2013|newspaper=Nashville Music Guide|date=October 2, 2010}}</ref> He has since been nominated for the Canadian Country Music Association Rising Star Award in both 2011 and 2012. Prevost was selected as one of the Top 12 out of 7,500 artists in the world in the Unsigned Only Competition.<ref>{{cite news|title=Canadian Artists Honored In US-Based Unsigned Only Music Competition|url=http://www.thebeatmagazine.ca/index.php/featured-posts/1248-canadian-artists-honored-in-us-based-unsigned-only-music-competition|archive-url=https://archive.today/20130915165342/http://www.thebeatmagazine.ca/index.php/featured-posts/1248-canadian-artists-honored-in-us-based-unsigned-only-music-competition|url-status=dead|archive-date=September 15, 2013|accessdate=September 15, 2013|newspaper=The Beat Magazine}}</ref> He has four official music videos on [[CMT (Canada)|CMT]], the most recent video being his latest single "Last Night All Day." He has been featured on ''[[Entertainment Tonight Canada|ET Canada]]'' and has opened for [[Joe Nichols]], [[The Oak Ridge Boys]] and [[Florida Georgia Line]]. He was nominated at the CCMA Awards for Interactive Artist of the Year in 2013.<ref>{{cite news|last=Nicholson|first=Jessica|title=Canadian Country Music Awards Nominees Announced|url=http://www.musicrow.com/2013/07/canadian-country-music-awards-nominees-announced/|accessdate=September 15, 2013|newspaper=[[MusicRow]]|date=July 17, 2013}}</ref>
