'''Joanna Janét''' (born in [[Baton Rouge, Louisiana]]<ref name="whitburn">{{cite book|last=Whitburn|first=Joel|title=Hot Country Songs 1944 to 2008|publisher=Record Research, Inc|page=206|year=2008|isbn=978-0-89820-177-2}}</ref>) is an American [[country music]] singer. Janét was signed to [[DreamWorks Records|DreamWorks Nashville]] and recorded an album for the label, ''Destination Love'', that was scheduled to be released in August 2002.<ref>{{cite web|title=JoAnna Janét Biography|url=http://www.wma.com/joanna_janet/bio/JoAnna_Janet.pdf|work=[[William Morris Endeavor]]|accessdate=July 11, 2011}}</ref> The first single from the album, "Since I've Seen You Last," peaked at number 55 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Country Songs|Hot Country Singles & Tracks]] chart.<ref name="whitburn" /> When Janét's producer, [[Paul Worley]], left DreamWorks for [[Warner Bros. Records]], she went with him.<ref>{{cite web|title=JoAnna Janét – Songs Worth Keeping|url=http://www.yamaha.com/publications/allaccess/winter2003/02janet.htm|work=[[Yamaha Corporation]]|accessdate=July 11, 2011|year=2003|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110615114243/http://www.yamaha.com/publications/allaccess/winter2003/02janet.htm|archive-date=June 15, 2011|url-status=dead}}</ref>
