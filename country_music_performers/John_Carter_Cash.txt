'''John Carter Cash''' (born March 3, 1970) is an American [[country music|country]] singer-songwriter, musician and author.  He is the only child of [[Johnny Cash]] and his second wife [[June Carter Cash]]. He is the grandson of [[Maybelle Carter|Mother Maybelle Carter]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.countrythangdaily.com/john-carter-cash/|title=Get To Know The Man in Black's Multi-Talented Son, John Carter Cash|date=April 1, 2021}}</ref>

==Biography==
[[File:Johnny Cash and Son 1975.jpg|right|thumb|upright|With his father, {{circa}} 1975]]

John has four older half-sisters from his father's first marriage to [[Vivian Cash]] (née Liberto). They are [[Rosanne Cash|Rosanne]], Kathy, [[Cindy Cash|Cindy]], and Tara.

He also has two older half-sisters from his mother's previous marriages, singers [[Carlene Carter]] and Rosie Nix.

For several years after John's birth, his father altered the conclusion of "[[A Boy Named Sue]]" to mention the boy by name. In 1972 his parents recorded the duet "I Got a Boy (And His Name is John)" about their son. In 1975 a photograph of him with his father was featured on the sleeve of the album ''[[Look at Them Beans]]''.{{citation needed|date=April 2022}}

Like many of his siblings, John Cash followed his parents into show business. He has worked as a singer-songwriter, musician, and producer. He was music producer on his mother's albums ''[[Press On (June Carter Cash album)|Press On]]'' and ''[[Wildwood Flower (album)|Wildwood Flower]]''; the latter won a [[Grammy Awards|Grammy Award]] for Best Traditional Folk album.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/wildwood-flower-mw0000042126/credits|title=Wildwood Flower - June Carter Cash &#124; Credits &#124; AllMusic|via=www.allmusic.com}}</ref>

He was the associate producer of his father's ''[[American III: Solitary Man]]'' and ''[[American IV: The Man Comes Around]]''. In the early 1990s he toured with his father as a rhythm guitarist.  

He has also produced recordings for such artists as: [[Sheryl Crow]], [[Loretta Lynn]], [[Willie Nelson]], [[Elvis Costello]], [[Kris Kristofferson]], [[Emmylou Harris]], and [[Vince Gill]]. He owns and operates Cash Cabin Enterprises, LLC., a music production company in the Cash Cabin Studio.<ref name="autopbs.org">{{Cite web|url=https://www.pbs.org/kenburns/country-music/john-carter-cash-biography|title=John Carter Cash Biography|website=Country Music}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/john-carter-cash-mn0000811962/credits|title=John Carter Cash &#124; Credits|website=AllMusic}}</ref>

==Marriage and family==

By 1999, John Carter Cash had met fiddler [[Laura Weber White|Laura Weber]] when she joined his parents' ''[[Press On (June Carter Cash album)|Press On]]'' tour to promote his mother’s album of the same name. John Carter Cash and Laura Weber were married in 2000.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.junecartercash.com/timeline/|title=Timeline|website=June Carter Cash Official Site}}</ref> They had two children, Anna Maybelle Cash (nicknamed AnnaBelle) and Jack Ezra Cash.<ref name="automandolincafe">{{Cite web|url=https://www.mandolincafe.com/news/publish/mandolins_001243.shtml|title=10 Questions for Laura Weber Cash|website=Mandolin Cafe}}</ref><ref>[https://sckool.org/the-story-of-the-oregon-old-time-fiddlers-association.html?page=11  	 The story of the Oregon Old-time Fiddlers Association]</ref><ref>[https://www.coloradofiddlers.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Laura-Weber-Cash-Bio.pdf Colorado Old Time Fiddlers' Association, Laura Weber Cash]</ref><ref name="cash">{{Cite web |title=It's a boy for the Cash family |url=https://www.countrystandardtime.com/news/newsitem.asp?xid=11880 |access-date=2022-03-13 |website=www.countrystandardtime.com}}</ref> They later divorced in 2013.

He later married [[Ana Cristina Cash]], a singer, in 2016 in [[Charleston, South Carolina]]. They have two children, Grace June<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theboot.com/john-carter-cash-ana-cristina-cash-baby-girl/ |website=theboot.com |title=John Carter + Ana Cristina Cash Welcome Baby Girl |author=Angela Stefano |date= September 18, 2017 |access-date=May 30, 2021}}</ref> and James Kristoffer.<ref name="expect"/><ref name="cash"/> 

Cash also has one older son, Joseph John, from another previous marriage.<ref name="cash">{{Cite web |title=It's a boy for the Cash family |url=https://www.countrystandardtime.com/news/newsitem.asp?xid=11880 |access-date=2022-03-13 |website=www.countrystandardtime.com}}</ref><ref name="expect">[https://theboot.com/john-carter-cash-ana-cristina-cash-baby-girl-2017/ John Carter Cash, Wife Ana Cristina Expecting a Baby Girl] TheBoot.Com by Angela Stefano. Published: April 14, 2017. Accessed August 16, 2022</ref>

== Books ==
Cash has written books about his parents:
* ''Anchored in Love: an intimate portrait of June Carter Cash'', Thomas Nelson, 2007
* ''House of Cash: the legacies of my father Johnny Cash'', Insight Editions, 2011
* ''Cash and Carter family cookbook: recipes and recollections from Johnny and June's table'', Thomas Nelson, 2018

He also has written children's books:
* ''Momma Loves Her Little Son'', Little Simon Inspirations, 2009
* ''Daddy Loves His Little Girl'', Little Simon Inspirations, 2010
* ''The Cat in the Rhinestone Suit'', Little Simon Inspirations, 2012
* ''Lupus Rex'', a novel, Ravenstone, 2013.
