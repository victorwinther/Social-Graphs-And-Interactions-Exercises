'''Sinead Burgess''' (born 10 November 1990) is a Sydney-based singer-songwriter signed to ABC Music and [[Island Records Australia]], which is part of [[Universal Music Australia]].<ref name="New_Single">{{cite web | url= http://www.couriermail.com.au/questnews/moreton/beachmere-singer-songwriter-sinead-burgess-to-launch-new-single/story-fn8m0yu3-1226424684402?nk=e6caa6ae1398056864520c2611cf2a2d | title = Beachmere singer-songwriter Sinead Burgess to launch new single | date = 15 July 2012 | publisher = The Courier Mail | accessdate = 30 July 2014 }}</ref> Burgess first began her singing career in 2007 and subsequently released a 5-track EP titled ''You Get Me''; featuring the title track and "Butterflies" which were released as singles and in many compilation albums across Australia. Burgess's single "Goodnight America" was co-written with UK pop producer Stuart Crichton who has worked with [[Kylie Minogue]], [[Pet Shop Boys]], Bond and [[The Sugababes]].<ref name="Goodnight_America">{{cite web | url= http://www.vmusic.com.au/features/sinead-burgess-video-premiere.aspx | title = Sinead Burgess Video Premiere | date = 2012 | publisher = V Music | accessdate = 30 July 2014 }}</ref> Burgess supported Irish band The Script at a one-off show in [[Sydney]] in 2012.<ref name="Gig">{{cite web | url= http://www.sunshinecoastdaily.com.au/news/sinead-mirrors-cool-katy-sinead-follows-script/1811736/ | title = Sinead mirrors cool Katy | date = 30 March 2013 | publisher = Sunshine Coast Daily | accessdate = 4 August 2014 }}</ref>

Burgess released her single "Rearview Mirrow" digitally in 2013 and then worked on a 6-Track EP titled ''Wolf'', released as a digital download in 2016.  Burgess since toured with [[The Shires (duo)]] on their [[Accidentally on Purpose (The Shires album)|Accidentally On Purpose]] tour as a support act.

She released the album ''Damaged Goods'' on 17 August 2018.
