'''Seth Ennis''' (born November 30, 1992) is an American [[country music]] singer. He has released one single for [[Arista Nashville]].

==History==
Ennis was born in Dothan, Alabama into a military family but he calls Valdosta, Georgia home. He played drums and piano from an early age.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/seth-ennis-mn0003518022/biography|title=Seth Ennis|last=Monger|first=James Christopher|work=Allmusic|accessdate=18 April 2017}}</ref>

After moving back from Georgia to Nashville in 2013, Ennis signed up for a Battle of the Bands contest, which he won. He also wrote [[Tyler Farr]]'s 2016 single "Our Town".<ref name="taste">{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/seth-ennis-woke-up-in-nashville-interview/|title=How Hard Work Paid Off for Seth Ennis|last=Reuter|first=Annie|work=[[Taste of Country]]|accessdate=18 April 2017}}</ref> He signed with [[Arista Nashville]] in September 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.musicrow.com/2016/09/arista-nashville-signs-seth-ennis-releases-woke-up-in-nashville/|title=Arista Nashville Signs Seth Ennis, Releases "Woke Up In Nashville"|last=Nicholson|first=Jessica|date=September 6, 2016|work=MusicRow|accessdate=18 April 2017}}</ref>

Arista released his debut single "Woke Up in Nashville", which he wrote with Blair Daly and David Hodges. The song was produced by Corey Crowder, and features Ennis playing all of the instruments himself.<ref name="taste"/>

In 2017, he appeared at the [[C2C: Country to Country]] festival in the UK and later supported [[Little Big Town]] on the UK leg of their [[The Breakers Tour]].
