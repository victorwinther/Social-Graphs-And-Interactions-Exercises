'''Wyatt''' is a Canadian [[country music]] group from [[Saskatoon, Saskatchewan]] composed of Scott Patrick (vocals, guitar), Daniel Fortier (vocals, guitar), Bray Hudson (drums) and Cam Ewart (bass).<ref>[http://www.cmt.ca/Artist/wwwwyattmusiccom Wyatt Biography]</ref> Following the release of a Christmas album, ''Snowed In'', in 2005, Wyatt released their debut album, ''Hard Road'', in 2007.<ref name="leaderpost">{{cite web |last=Tessmer |first=Christopher |title=Wyatt's prepared to ride on |url=http://www2.canada.com/reginaleaderpost/news/whats_on/story.html?id=39ffbfe7-251b-4e1e-9bfa-8338dee026ff |work=[[Regina Leader-Post]] |date=2010-01-14 |accessdate=2010-02-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120606153510/http://www2.canada.com/reginaleaderpost/news/whats_on/story.html?id=39ffbfe7-251b-4e1e-9bfa-8338dee026ff |archive-date=2012-06-06 |url-status=dead }}</ref> No singles were released from the project because the band "didn't feel it was quite there yet."<ref name="leaderpost" />

In June 2009, Wyatt won [[CHBD-FM|Big Dog 92.7]]'s The Next Big Thing talent contest. Their prize included $5,000 cash, a showcase for music industry professionals, career guidance, a trip to the [[Canadian Country Music Association]] Awards and the option of having a professional single released to radio.<ref name="leaderpost" /> Their single "Ride On" debuted at No.&nbsp;48 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' Canadian Country Singles chart in December 2009.<ref name="leaderpost" /> A second single, "Questions," reached the Top 40 in May 2010.<ref name="leaderpost2">{{cite web |last=Maciag |first=Samantha |title=Wyatt making its mark on radio stations across Canada |url=https://leaderpost.com/entertainment/Wyatt+making+mark+radio+stations+across+Canada/3105616/story.html |work=[[Regina Leader-Post]] |date=2010-06-04 |accessdate=2010-08-06}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref> Both songs are included on Wyatt's second studio album, ''If I Had a Dollar…'', released in June 2010.<ref name="leaderpost2" />
