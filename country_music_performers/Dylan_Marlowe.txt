'''Dylan Marlowe''' is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[Statesboro, Georgia]] signed to [[Sony Music Nashville]].<ref name="Sony Signing"/>

==Background==
Marlowe relocated to [[Nashville, Tennessee]] shortly before the start of the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] and signed a publishing and artist development deal with Play It Again Entertainment in 2020.<ref name="Sony Signing"/> Following several self-released songs, a growing social media presence, and scoring his first Number One hit as a songwriter with [[Jon Pardi]]'s "[[Last Night Lonely]]",<ref name="Holler">{{cite web|url=https://holler.country/news/breaking/exclusive-dylan-marlowe-on-authenticity-his-debut-album-boys-back-home-and-more/|title=Exclusive: Dylan Marlowe on Authenticity, His Debut Album, 'Boys Back Home' and More|author=Maxim Mower|date=January 10, 2024|website=Holler|access-date=August 9, 2024}}</ref> it was announced in January 2023, that Marlowe had signed a recording contract with [[Sony Music Nashville]].<ref name="Sony Signing">{{cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2023/01/sony-music-nashville-signs-dylan-marlowe/|title=Sony Music Nashville Signs Dylan Marlowe|author=LB Cantrell|date=January 27, 2023|website=Music Row|access-date=August 9, 2024}}</ref> His debut EP with the label, ''Dirt Road When I Die'', was released on July 7, 2023.<ref name="EP"/> Marlowe's debut single to country radio, "Boys Back Home" (a duet with [[Dylan Scott]]), was released on December 4, 2023.<ref name="Aircheck">{{cite web|url=https://assets.countryaircheck.com/public/9a096aad-e54d-4456-8d6f260485724392/Issue-887-December-4-2023.pdf|title=Country Aircheck Weekly December 4, 2023, Issue 887|date=December 4, 2023|website=Country Aircheck|accessdate=August 9, 2024}}</ref> Originally recorded in 2021, its release was delayed due to Scott's ''[[Livin' My Best Life]]'', and Marlowe had to later re-record his vocals when the label decided to issue it as his debut single instead of "Record High".<ref name="Holler"/> Marlowe's debut studio album, ''Mid-Twenties Crisis'', will be released on September 27, 2024.<ref name="Debut">{{cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2024/07/dylan-marlowe-will-explore-mid-twenties-crisis-on-debut-album/|title=Dylan Marlowe Will Explore 'Mid-Twenties Crisis' On Debut Album|author=Madison Hahnen|date=July 26, 2024|website=Music Row|access-date=August 9, 2024}}</ref>
