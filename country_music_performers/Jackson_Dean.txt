'''Jackson Dean Nicholson''' (born October 6, 2000) is an American [[country music]] singer and songwriter. He signed to [[Big Machine Records]] in 2021 and has charted with the singles "[[Don't Come Lookin']]" and "Fearless (The Echo)".

==Biography==
Dean was born in [[Odenton, Maryland]]. He left his hometown at age 18 to begin songwriting, which led to collaborations with [[Casey Beathard]] and Luke Dick. After serving as an opening act for [[Kane Brown]], [[Jake Owen]], and [[Brothers Osborne]], he was signed to [[Big Machine Records]] in 2021.<ref name="allmusic">{{cite web | url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/jackson-dean-mn0003569404/biography | title=Jackson Dean biography | publisher=AllMusic | accessdate=April 19, 2022}}</ref> Dean's first release for Big Machine was a self-titled [[extended play]] with the single "Wings", the former of which was released on April 16, 2021.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://musicrow.com/2021/04/jackson-dean-announces-debut-project-on-big-machine-records/ | title=Jackson Dean Announces Debut Project On Big Machine Records | publisher=MusicRow | date=April 16, 2021 | accessdate=April 19, 2022}}</ref>

Dean's debut single, "[[Don't Come Lookin']]", was released in February 2022, followed by his debut album, ''Greenbroke'', on March 11, 2022. In addition, he was booked as an opening act for [[Brooks & Dunn]]'s Reboot 2022 Tour.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://tasteofcountry.com/jackson-dean-greenbroke-debut-album-dont-come-looking/ | title=Jackson Dean Announces Debut Album, Shares First Single | publisher=[[Taste of Country]] | date=February 28, 2022 | accessdate=April 19, 2022 | author=Carena Liptak}}</ref> "Don't Come Lookin'" peaked at number three on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Country Airplay]] chart in November. The song was also featured in an episode of the [[Paramount Network]] TV series ''[[Yellowstone (American TV series)|Yellowstone]]''.<ref name="allmusic"/> "Fearless (The Echo)" was released as the second single from ''Greenbroke'' in 2023, and it reached a peak of number 11 on the ''Billboard'' Country Airplay chart in 2024.

Dean's second studio album, ''On the Back of My Dreams'', will be released on September 6, 2024.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://americansongwriter.com/jackson-dean-announces-sophomore-studio-album-on-back-dreams-new-single-heavens-betsy/|title=Jackson Dean Announces Sophomore Studio Album 'On the Back of My Dreams' with New Single "Heavens to Betsy"|author=Clayton Edwards|date=August 2, 2024|website=American Songwriter|access-date=August 2, 2024}}</ref> Its lead single, "Heavens to Betsy", was released in August 2024.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://assets.countryaircheck.com/public/0c45c46f-2b9a-440e-9c58e60e885345d1/Issue-920-July-29-2024.pdf|title=Country Aircheck Weekly July 29, 2024, Issue 920|date=July 29, 2024|website=Country Aircheck|accessdate=July 30, 2024}}</ref>
