'''Houston County''' was an American [[country music]] trio formerly signed to [[Stroudavarious Records]]. The group is composed of brothers Zack Hooper ([[lead vocals]]) and Adam Hooper ([[bass guitar]]), as well as John Milldrum ([[lead guitar]]).<ref name="engine145"/> The trio released its debut single "I Can't Make It Rain" in September 2009, and it debuted at No.&nbsp;58 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Country Songs]] chart dated for the week of October 31, 2009. Karlie Justus of Engine 145 gave the song a thumbs-up, saying that it had "weak opening chords" but that it was "lyrically interesting."<ref name="engine145">{{cite web |author=Karlie Justus |title=Houston County â€” "I Can't Make It Rain" |url=http://www.engine145.com/houston-county-i-cant-make-it-rain/ |work=Engine 145 |date=2009-09-03 |accessdate=2009-10-19 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141218105927/http://www.engine145.com/houston-county-i-cant-make-it-rain/ |archivedate=2014-12-18 }}</ref> The band has also filmed a [[music video]] for this song.<ref name="eagle">
