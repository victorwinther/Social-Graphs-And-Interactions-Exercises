'''The Lacs''' is an American [[country rap]] duo that consists of Robert Clayton (Clay) "Uncle Snap" Sharpe and Brian Andrew "Rooster" King.<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-615/5679824/the-lacs-score-career-best-rank-on-top-country-albums|title=The Lacs' Score Career-Best Rank On Top Country Albums|magazine=Billboard|access-date=May 3, 2016}}</ref> At first, the group also featured Brandon Herndon on the three self-released albums on Side Ya Mouth Records.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.showcaseyourmusic.com/LACS|title=SYM – LACS|website=Showcase Your Music|access-date=May 3, 2016}}</ref>

The Lacs have recorded six albums for Backroad Records, a subsidiary of [[Average Joes Entertainment]], an independent label co-owned by fellow country rap artist [[Colt Ford]]. Four albums have entered the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]: ''Keep it Redneck'' (2013), ''Outlaw in Me'' and ''American Rebelution'' (2017).

The Lacs created their own record label ''Dirt Rock Empire''<ref name="dirtrocksite">{{cite web |url=http://www.dirtrockempire.com/ |title=DirtRockEmpire.com |work=DirRockEmpire }}</ref> in 2017. On September 22, 2017, The Lacs released the first album from Dirt Rock titled ''Dirtbagz, Vol. 1'' which is a compilation album featuring many of the labels artists such as Nate Kenyon, Crucifix and Hard Target.
