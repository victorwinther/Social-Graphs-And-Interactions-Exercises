'''High Valley''' is a Canadian [[Country music|country]] and [[bluegrass music|bluegrass]] band originally from Blumenort, Alberta, a small community near the hamlet of [[La Crete]]. The group is composed of Brad Rempel (lead vocals) and his supporting band, Dave Myers (bass guitar), Raymond Klassen (Dobro), Clint Milburn (guitar), and Andrew Hemmerling (drums).<ref>{{cite web | url=https://musicrow.com/2021/11/high-valley-reemerges-with-new-music-due-out-nov-12/ | title=High Valley Reemerges with New Music Due Out Nov. 12 | date=4 November 2021 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.countryschatter.com/2016/09/high-valley-know-how-to-turn-a-show-into-a-party/ | title=High Valley know how to turn a show into a party | date=13 September 2016 }}</ref> Brad Rempel's brother Bryan Rempel was a member until March 2014,<ref>{{cite news|last=Tinson|first=Kayla|title=Bryan Rempel leaving High Valley|url=http://topcountry.ca/bryan-rempel-leaving-high-valley/|access-date=March 12, 2014|newspaper=Top Country|date=March 10, 2014}}</ref> while younger brother Curtis Rempel was a member until June 2021.<ref>{{cite web|first=Katie|last=Colley|title=Curtis Rempel Announces Exit From Country Duo High Valley To Focus On New Business|url=https://etcanada.com/news/790741/curtis-rempel-announces-exit-from-country-duo-high-valley-to-focus-on-new-business/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210611154717/https://etcanada.com/news/790741/curtis-rempel-announces-exit-from-country-duo-high-valley-to-focus-on-new-business/|url-status=dead|archive-date=June 11, 2021|work=[[Entertainment Tonight Canada|ET Canada]]|date=June 11, 2021}}</ref> All three brothers grew up in a [[Mennonite]] community and graduated from La Crete Public School.<ref name="cmt">[http://www.cmt.ca/Artist/HighValley High Valley] at [[CMT (Canada)|CMT]]</ref>

After a number of chart successes in Canada between 2010 and 2015, High Valley were signed to [[Atlantic Records]] Nashville in October 2015.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Nicholson|first1=Jessica|title=High Valley Inks Deal With Atlantic Records/Warner Music Nashville|url=http://www.musicrow.com/2015/10/high-valley-inks-deal-with-atlantic-recordswarner-music-nashville/|access-date=October 9, 2015|work=[[MusicRow]]|date=October 8, 2015}}</ref> They achieved some success with the singles "[[Make You Mine (High Valley song)|Make You Mine]]" and "[[She's with Me]]" at [[United States]] country radio. They have three No.&nbsp;1 hits on the ''Billboard'' [[Canada Country]] chart with "[[I Be U Be]]", "[[Grew Up On That]]", and "[[River's Still Running]]".
