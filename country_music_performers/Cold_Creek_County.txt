''' Cold Creek County '''is a Canadian [[country rock]] group based in [[Brighton, Ontario|Brighton]], [[Ontario]]. Its members are Doug Oliver, Josh Lester, Trevor MacLeod, Justin Lester, and Jordan Honsinger. They were founded in 2013<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bencic|first1=Sarah|title=Local Tunes: Cold Creek County|url=http://trentarthur.ca/local-tunes-cold-creek-county/|accessdate=June 28, 2015|work=Arthur|date=January 20, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=A rocking good time was had by all|url=http://www.insidebelleville.com/whatson-story/4198754-a-rocking-good-time-was-had-by-all/|accessdate=June 28, 2015|work=Inside Belleville|date=November 7, 2013}}</ref> and signed to [[Sony Music Canada]] in 2014.<ref name="thatscountry">{{cite news|title=Cold Creek County's Debut Single "Our Town" #1 Most Added – All Formats|url=http://thatscountry.com/2015/05/14/cold-creek-countys-debut-single-our-town-1-most-added-all-formats/|accessdate=June 28, 2015|work=That's Country|date=May 14, 2015}}</ref><ref name="cmt">{{cite web|title=Cold Creek County Biography|url=http://www.cmt.ca/artist/cold-creek-county|website=[[CMT (Canadian TV channel)|CMT]]|accessdate=June 28, 2015}}</ref>

==History==

They toured with [[Dallas Smith (singer)|Dallas Smith]], [[Emerson Drive]], [[Kira Isabella]] and [[Jason Blaine]].<ref name="cmt" /> They released their debut single, "Our Town", in April 2015.<ref>{{cite news|title=Cold Creek County Releases Debut Single "Our Town" Co-Written By Todd Clark|url=http://www.windupsongs.com/cold-creek-county-releases-debut-single-our-town-co-written-by-todd-clark/|accessdate=June 28, 2015|work=Wind-Up Songs|date=April 28, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150630141904/http://www.windupsongs.com/cold-creek-county-releases-debut-single-our-town-co-written-by-todd-clark/|archive-date=June 30, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> It was the most added debut single by a Canadian country artist.<ref name="thatscountry" /> It has reached the top 10 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Canada Country]] chart.<ref name="Canada Country">{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/biz/search/charts?f%5B0%5D=ts_chart_artistname%3ACold%20Creek%20County&f%5B1%5D=itm_field_chart_id%3A1240&f%5B2%5D=ss_bb_type%3Achart_item&type=2&artist=Cold%20Creek%20County|title=Cold Creek County Album & Song Chart History - Canada Country|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|publisher=[[Prometheus Global Media]]|accessdate=June 28, 2015}}</ref> It was written by Todd Clark and Gavin Slate.

The band performed in 2016 at the Tweed Stampede and Jamboree.<ref>[https://www.intelligencer.ca/2016/08/01/photos-rodeo-in-tweed/wcm/c7fc9cb1-5833-b8e9-d965-c056c4142fe4 "PHOTOS: Rodeo in Tweed"]. ''The Belleville Intelligencer'', August 1, 2016</ref> That year the band was presented with a Canadian Radio Music Award.<ref>[https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7357941/crmas-2016-canadian-radio-music-awards-dance-the-weeknd-alessia-cara "The Weeknd, Alessia Cara & More Score at 2016 Canadian Radio Music Awards"]. ''BillBoard'', 5/6/2016 by Matt Medved</ref>

In June, 2019 Cold Creek Country performed at the Country Music Association of Ontario awards gala.<ref>[https://www.fyimusicnews.ca/articles/2019/06/17/meghan-patrick-triumphant-cmaontario-awards "Meghan Patrick Triumphant At CMAOntario Awards"]. ''FYI Music News'', Jun 17, 2019.</ref> On September 24, 2019, Josh Lester and his brother Justin left the band.
