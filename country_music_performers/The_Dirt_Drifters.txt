'''The Dirt Drifters''' was an American [[country music]] group made up of five musicians: lead singer/guitarist Matt Fleener, vocalist/guitarist Ryan Fleener, vocalist/guitarist Jeff Middleton, bassist Jeremy Little and drummer Nick Diamond.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thedirtdrifters.com/bio/|title=Bio|work=The Dirt Drifters|accessdate=August 10, 2011}}</ref> They were signed to [[Warner Bros. Records]] and their debut CD, ''This Is My Blood'', was released on July 19, 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/the-dirt-drifters-interview-2011/|title=The Dirt Drifters Will Always Have a 'Reason' for Making Country Music|last=Conaway|first=Alanna|date=June 29, 2011|work=Taste of Country|accessdate=July 18, 2011}}</ref>

Their debut single, "Something Better," was released in February 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theboot.com/2011/03/04/the-dirt-drifters-interview/|title=Dirt Drifters Bring 'Something Better' to Country Music|last=Duvall|first=Erin|date=March 4, 2011|work=The Boot|accessdate=March 10, 2011}}</ref>
