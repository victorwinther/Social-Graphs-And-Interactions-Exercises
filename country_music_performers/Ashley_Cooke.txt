'''Ashley Cooke''' (born June 12, 1997) is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[Parkland, Florida]] signed to [[Big Loud]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://ashleycooke.com/|title=Ashley Cooke Official Website|accessdate=November 4, 2023}}</ref>

==Background==
Cooke independently released ''Already Drank That Beer - Side A'' in 2021.<ref name="ashley cooke talks radio">{{cite web|url=https://www.cmt.com/news/tk7gcj/ashley-cooke-talks-radio-single-never-til-now-with-brett-young-and-becoming-a-signed-artist|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220817190927/https://www.cmt.com/news/tk7gcj/ashley-cooke-talks-radio-single-never-til-now-with-brett-young-and-becoming-a-signed-artist|url-status=dead|archive-date=August 17, 2022|title=Ashley Cooke Talks Radio Single "Never Til Now" With Brett Young, Becoming a Signed Artist|author=Tiffany Goldstein|date=July 18, 2022|website=CMT|accessdate=November 6, 2023}}</ref> The album was later retitled as ''Already Drank That Beer'' and reissued on April 1, 2022, adding a new duet version of "Never Til Now" which featured [[Brett Young (singer)|Brett Young]]. It was released as Cooke's debut single in May 2022 and promoted by [[Big Loud]] who she inked a recording contract with.<ref name="ashley cooke talks radio"/> It peaked at number 49 on the ''Billboard'' [[Country Airplay]] chart. 

''Shot in the Dark'', Cooke's 24-track second studio and major label debut album, was released on July 21, 2023, via Big Loud.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2023/06/ashley-cooke-to-take-a-shot-in-the-dark-on-july-21/|title=Ashley Cooke To Take A 'Shot In The Dark' On July 21|author=Lorie Hollabaugh|date=July 21, 2023|website=Music Row|accessdate=November 6, 2023}}</ref> It reprises Cooke's debut single "Never Til Now" and also features new collaborations with [[Colbie Caillat]], [[Nate Smith (singer)|Nate Smith]], and [[Jackson Dean]]. "Your Place" was released on December 11, 2023, as the album's lead single,<ref>{{cite web | url=https://musicrow.com/single-track-releases/ | title=Single/Track Releases }}</ref> and Cooke was the recipient of [[iHeartRadio]]'s On the Verge program.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.instagram.com/p/C0uZ8rEO2be/ | title=Instagram }}</ref>
