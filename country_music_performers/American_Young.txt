'''American Young''' is an American [[country music]] duo signed to [[Curb Records]]. The duo is composed of Kristy Osmunson, formerly of the group [[Bomshel]], and songwriter/producer Jon Stone.

In late 2013, the duo released their debut single, "Love Is War", which has charted on [[Country Airplay]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://music.yahoo.com/blogs/our-country/meet-country-duo-american-young--familiar-faces--new-sound-203059879.html|title=Meet Country Duo American Young: Familiar Faces, New Sound|last=Geller|first=Wendy|date=5 December 2013|work=[[Yahoo! Music]]|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref> Billy Dukes of [[Taste of Country]] gave the song 4 out of 5 stars, comparing their sound favorably to [[The Civil Wars]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/american-young-love-is-war/|title='Love Is War' review|last=Dukes|first=Billy|date=15 August 2013|work=Taste of Country|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>
