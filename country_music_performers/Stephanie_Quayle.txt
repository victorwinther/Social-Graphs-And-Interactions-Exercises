'''Stephanie Quayle''' (born October 15, 1979) is an American singer-songwriter and musician.

==Early life==
Stephanie Quayle grew up in [[Bozeman, Montana]]. She began playing piano at the age of 4, bought a guitar at 15, and the following year began singing in a band and found her calling on stage.<ref name="sal">{{Citation|title=Singer Stephanie Quayle determined to give back to community that 'grew' her|date=Sep 25, 2015|url=http://www.bozemandailychronicle.com/go/music/country-roots-singer-stephanie-quayle-determined-to-give-back-to/article_c5aa45e6-d698-5831-9ccf-d44e256408fd.html|author=Rachel Hergett|publisher=Bozeman Daily Chronicle}}</ref>

==Career==
===2016–2018: ''Love the Way You See Me''===
In June 2016, Quayle released "Drinking with Dolly" via Rebel Engine Entertainment.<ref name="countryradioseminar.com">{{cite web|url=http://countryradioseminar.com/events/rebel-engine-charging-and-giveaway-depot-1|title=Rebel Engine Charging & Giveaway Depot - countryradioseminar.com|website=countryradioseminar.com}}</ref> It was written by [[Victoria Banks]] and [[Rachel Proctor]]. The music video topped the CMT Music 12 Pack<ref name="thecountrynote.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.thecountrynote.com/in-the-news/stephanie-quayle-makes-her-debut-on-fox-friends-friday-december-16/|title=Stephanie Quayle Makes Her Debut On Fox & Friends Friday, December 16 - The Country Note|date=15 December 2016|publisher=}}</ref> while the single peaked in the Top 50 of the ''Billboard'' Indicator Chart. Quayle made her national television debut performing on ''Fox & Friends'' in December 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://websterpr.com/news/stephanie-quayle-makes-her-debut-fox-friends-friday-december-16|title=STEPHANIE QUAYLE MAKES HER DEBUT ON FOX & FRIENDS FRIDAY DECEMBER 16|website=websterpr.com}}</ref>

In March 2017, her single "Winnebago," was released to radio, with the music video airing on GAC, CMT, and The Country Network. [[Winnebago Industries|Winnebago]] teamed up as a partner on her tour, along with The RV Loft and KOA<ref name="countryradioseminar.com"/> where Quayle traveled across the US from the Empire State Building to the Golden Gate, living out the lyrics of the song.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/7631675/stephanie-quayle|title=Stephanie Quayle|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |publisher=}}</ref>

''Rolling Stone'' named Quayle "Top 10 Artists To Watch" in May 2017<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/country/lists/10-new-country-artists-you-need-to-know-may-2017-w481172/stephanie-quayle-w481177|title=10 New Country Artists You Need to Know: May 2017|magazine=Rolling Stone|date=9 May 2017 }}</ref> and she has shared the stage with artists including Chase Rice, Granger Smith, LoCash, Trace Adkins, and Gary Allan.<ref name="thecountrynote.com"/>

Rebel Engine Entertainment released her album ''Love The Way You See Me'' on September 1, 2017, with an exclusive "first" listen with People Country.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.com/country/stephanie-quayle-love-the-way-you-see-me-album-premiere/|title=Stephanie Quayle Surprises Her 'Flock' of Fans by Releasing New Album a Week Early: Listen Here|date=31 August 2017|publisher=}}</ref> The album's third single, "Selfish," was released in late 2017, and became her first single to chart on the ''Billboard'' [[Country Airplay]] chart when it debuted at number 60 for the week dated April 14, 2018.

Quayle was the only independent artist invited to perform for the five living former US presidents (Barack Obama, George W. Bush, Bill Clinton, George H.W. Bush, and Jimmy Carter) alongside artists like Lady Gaga and Alabama as part of the hurricane relief benefit concert, "Deep from the Heart: The One America Appeal" in Texas.<ref>{{cite web |title=FORMER PRESIDENTS OBAMA, BUSH 43, CLINTON, BUSH 41 AND CARTER TO HEADLINE OCT 21ST CONCERT BENEFITING HURRICANE RELIEF EFFORTS |url=https://www.oneamericaappeal.org/files/2017-10-21-Deep_from_the_Heart.pdf}}</ref>

On April 7, 2018, Quayle made her [[Grand Ole Opry]] debut.

In November 2018, Quayle was inducted into CMT's 2019 Next Women of Country class.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/cmt-2019-next-women-of-country/|title=CMT Announces Next Women of Country Class of 2019|last=Houghton|first=Cillea|website=Taste of Country|date=24 November 2018 |language=en|access-date=2019-01-16}}</ref>

===2019–present: ''If I Was a Cowboy'' ===
Quayle's highly anticipated EP ''If I Was a Cowboy'' was released on October 4, 2019. Rebel Engine Entertainment released the stripped-down version of the ''If I Was a Cowboy'' EP and "My Home's in Montana" in a video series, ''The Montana Sessions'' Presented by Wrangler, on February 14, 2020. She made her national daytime television debut performing "Whatcha Drinkin 'Bout" on ''The Kelly Clarkson Show''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://xlcountry.com/stephanie-quayle-on-the-kelly-clarkson-show-tuesday/|title=Stephanie Quayle on 'The Kelly Clarkson Show|last=Hart|first=Ally|website=XL Country|date=4 August 2020 |language=en|access-date=2020-09-16}}</ref>
