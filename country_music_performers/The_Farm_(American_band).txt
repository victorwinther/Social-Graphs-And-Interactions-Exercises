'''The Farm''', also known as '''The Farm Inc.''', was an American [[country music]] trio consisting of [[Nick Hoffman]] (vocals, fiddle), [[Damien Horne]] (vocals, keyboard, guitar) and [[Krista Marie]] (vocals, guitar). Signed to All In Records in association with Warner Music Nashville and [[Elektra Records]] and New Revolution, the trio has released the singles "[[Home Sweet Home (The Farm song)|Home Sweet Home]]" and "Be Grateful", which have charted on [[Hot Country Songs]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.countrystandardtime.com/news/newsitem2.asp?xid=5909|title=The Farm debut with 'Home Sweet Home'|date=21 November 2011|work=[[Country Standard Time]]|accessdate=29 November 2011}}</ref> Horne is a former member of [[John Rich (musician)|John Rich]]'s songwriting group [[MuzikMafia]], while Marie is a former solo artist for [[Broken Bow Records]]. Hoffman is the host and producer of the award-winning TV show "Nick's Wild Ride" that airs on [[Outdoor Channel]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.outdoorchannel.com/osa|title=OSA Landing Outdoor Sportsman Awards Fan Favorite Winners - Outdoor Channel|last=Channel|first=Outdoor|website=Outdoor Channel|language=en-US|access-date=2018-04-10}}</ref> and played fiddle in [[Kenny Chesney]]'s road band.<ref>{{cite journal|last=Nicholson|first=Jessica|date=16 January 2012|title=Who's New: The Farm|journal=[[Country Weekly]]|volume=19|issue=3|page=18|issn=1074-3235}}</ref>  The three met during a songwriting session which included former [[Western Flyer]] member Danny Myrick, who also became the group's producer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.roughstock.com/blog/new-artist-spotlight-the-farm-find-their-home-as-a-trio|title=New Artist Spotlight: The Farm Find Their "Home" as a Trio|last=Bjorke|first=Matt|date=19 October 2011|work=Roughstock|accessdate=29 November 2011}}</ref>

Their [[The Farm (album)|self-titled debut album]], released in 2012, produced two top 40 hits on [[Country Airplay]]: "[[Home Sweet Home (The Farm song)|Home Sweet Home]]" and "Be Grateful". In 2014, they announced a third single, "Mud".
