'''Sean Stemaly''' is an American [[country music|country]] singer and songwriter. He was previously signed to [[Big Loud|Big Loud Records]], and released the album ''Product of a Small Town''.<ref>{{cite news|title=Big Loud Records Hit The Gas With New Signing Sean Stemaly, Releasing ‘Back On A Backroad’|url=https://yourlifeinasong.com/big-loud-records-hit-the-gas-with-new-signing-sean-stemaly-releasing-back-on-a-backroad|work=Your Life in a Song|accessdate=January 7, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Sean Stemaly Inks Deal with Big Loud Records & Drops New Single|first=Bradley|last=Curry|url=https://www.raisedrowdy.com/2019/08/22/sean-stemaly-inks-deal-with-big-loud-records-drops-new-single/|work=Raised Rowdy|date=August 22, 2019}}</ref>

==Biography==
Stemaly was born in [[Western Kentucky]], and raised in Newburgh, IN.<ref name="Savannah">{{cite news|first=Sean|last=Kelly|title=Sean Stemaly’s Nashville epiphany |url=https://www.connectsavannah.com/savannah/sean-stemalys-nashville-epiphany/Content?oid=11421958|work=Connect Savannah|date=January 1, 2019}}</ref> Prior to pursuing a career in music, Stemaly worked for his father and grandfather who operate a land development company and a farm.<ref name="Savannah"/> In his teens, Stemaly taught himself to sing while driving a tractor.<ref>{{cite news|title=Country Next: Sean Stemaly|url=https://www.countrynow.com/country-next-sean-stemaly/|work=Country Now|first=Melinda|last=Lorge|date=January 30, 2020}}</ref> Stemaly cites [[Ronnie Dunn]], [[Tyler Childers]], and [[Luke Combs]] as musical influences.<ref name="Savannah"/>

In August 2019, Stemaly signed with [[Big Loud|Big Loud Records]] and released the track "Back on a Backroad", co-written by [[Hardy (singer)|Hardy]].<ref>{{cite news|title= Big Loud Signs New Artist Sean Stemaly|url=https://www.allaccess.com/net-news/archive/story/188803/big-loud-signs-new-artist-sean-stemaly|work=AllAccess|date=August 16, 2019}}</ref>
