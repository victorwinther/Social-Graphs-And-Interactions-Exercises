'''Jesse Mast''' is a Canadian [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[Salmon Arm]], British Columbia.<ref>{{cite web|title=Jesse Mast Biography|url=http://www.cmt.ca/artist/jesse-mast|website=[[CMT (Canadian TV channel)|CMT]]|accessdate=May 5, 2016}}</ref> He has written with Larry Wayne Clark and toured with [[Gord Bamford]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Peebles|first1=Frank|title=Mast hoists his flag in P.G.|url=http://www.princegeorgecitizen.com/entertainment/local-a-e/mast-hoists-his-flag-in-p-g-1.2228853|accessdate=May 5, 2016|work=[[The Prince George Citizen]]|date=April 11, 2016}}</ref> He won three awards from the North American Country Music Association International in 2014: Songwriter of the Year, Male Vocalist of the Year and Male Entertainer of the Year.<ref>{{cite news|title=Coldstream is the place to be this Victoria Day weekend|url=http://www.vernonmorningstar.com/entertainment/258700091.html|accessdate=May 5, 2016|work=[[The Morning Star (Vernon, British Columbia)|The Morning Star]]|date=May 11, 2014}}</ref>

Mast is signed to Cache Entertainment/[[Sony Music Canada]] and released his debut single, "Bad Blood", in January 2016.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bad Blood – Single|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/bad-blood-single/id1075413736|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=May 5, 2016}}</ref> It debuted on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Canada Country]] chart in March 2016.<ref name="Canada Country" />
