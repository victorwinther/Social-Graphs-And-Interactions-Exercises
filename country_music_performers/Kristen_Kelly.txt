'''Kristen Kelly''' is an American [[country music]] singer from [[Lorena, Texas]]. While signed to [[Arista Nashville]] she released her debut single, "Ex-Old Man". The song was the first from her self titled ep, produced by [[Tony Brown (record producer)|Tony Brown]] and [[Paul Overstreet]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aristanashville.com/artists/kristen-kelly |title=Kristen Kelly |publisher=Arista Nashville |date= |accessdate=2012-11-19}}</ref> "Ex-Old Man" was the number one most added debut song at country radio for a female artist and was Kelly’s first TOP 30 single. Radio Country Universe gave the song a B+, praising the sound as "back-to-basics".<ref>{{cite web|last=Foster |first=Ben |url=http://www.countryuniverse.net/2012/05/12/single-review-kristen-kelly-ex-old-man/ |title=Country Universe – A Country Music Blog » Single Review: Kristen Kelly, "Ex-Old Man" |publisher=Countryuniverse.net |date=2012-05-12 |accessdate=2012-11-19}}</ref> It debuted at number 60 on the [[Hot Country Songs]] chart dated May 12, 2012.<ref>{{cite web|author=Written by Edward Morris |url=http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1684475/rascal-flatts-banjo-rings-as-weeks-top-song.jhtml |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120508223130/http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1684475/rascal-flatts-banjo-rings-as-weeks-top-song.jhtml |url-status=dead |archive-date=May 8, 2012 |title=News : Rascal Flatts' "Banjo" Rings as Week's Top Song |publisher=CMT |date=2012-05-05 |accessdate=2012-11-19}}</ref>

That same year Kristen was a member of the first class of female artists inducted into CMT's Next Women of Country campaign which shines a spotlight on the newest ladies of country music.

Kelly has played the Grand Ole Opry and toured with some of country music's biggest names including [[Brad Paisley]]'s 2012 Virtual Reality World Tour<ref>{{cite web|author=|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/news-in-brief/1684507/brad-paisley-adds-three-newcomers-to-tour.jhtml |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120508095548/http://www.cmt.com/news/news-in-brief/1684507/brad-paisley-adds-three-newcomers-to-tour.jhtml |url-status=dead |archive-date=May 8, 2012 |title=News : Brad Paisley Adds Three Newcomers to Tour |publisher=CMT |date=2012-05-04 |accessdate=2012-11-19}}</ref>  Rascal Flatts, Alan Jackson, Sheryl Crow, The Band Perry, Willie Nelson, Gary Allan, Dierks Bentley, Jake Owen and more.

Kelly independently released her FIRE ep in 2015 and has released 3 new singles in 2020: “Ashes” which was on Season 7 episode 1 of the Netflix show The Ranch, “Country Music” featuring Craig Campbell and “Thank God For The Neon.”
