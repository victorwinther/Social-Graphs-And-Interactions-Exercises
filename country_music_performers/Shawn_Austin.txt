'''Shawn Austin''' (born January 13, 1989) is a Canadian [[country music|country]] singer and songwriter. He is signed to [[Big Loud Records]]' joint venture Local Hay.<ref>{{cite web|url= 
https://www.fyimusicnews.ca/articles/2021/08/12/music-news-digest-aug-12-2021|title=Music News Digest, Aug. 12, 2021|first=Kerry|last=Doole|work=FYI Music News|date=August 12, 2021}}</ref>

==Early life==
Born and raised in [[Vancouver]], [[British Columbia]], Austin elected to pursue a career in [[country music]] due to its "wholesomeness and authenticity", naming [[Garth Brooks]] as one of his influences. He stated [[Stevie Ray Vaughan]] was the reason he began playing the guitar, and that [[Michael Bublé]]'s melodies are an influence in his music as well.<ref name="YB">{{cite web|url=https://globalnews.ca/news/4568045/shawn-austin-interview/|title=Who is Shawn Austin? Introducing Canada's next big country star|first=Adam|last=Wells|date=October 18, 2018|work=[[Global News]]}}</ref>

==Career==
After debuting as an independent artist, Austin signed with [[Dallas Smith]]'s label Steelhead Music and released the single "Paradise Found" in 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2017/06/27/shawn-austin-releases-debut-single-paradise-found/|title=Shawn Austin releases debut single "Paradise Found"|first=Dutch|last=Bickell|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=June 27, 2017}}</ref> The song peaked at number eight on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Canada Country]] chart giving Austin a top ten hit to start his career.<ref name="CCW"/> He followed it up with the single "Get Me There" in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/music/shawn-austin-get-me-there/|title=Shawn Austin Releases New Single "Get Me There" Review|date=June 6, 2018|work=Front Porch Music}}</ref>

Later that year, Austin released "You Belong".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/music/you-belong-shawn-austin/|title=New Shawn Austin Single "You Belong"|date=November 8, 2018|work=Front Porch Music}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2018/09/04/shawn-austins-lead-single-you-belong-from-his-upcoming-self-titled-ep-is-released-everywhere-today/|title=Shawn Austin's lead single, "You Belong," from his upcoming self-titled EP is released everywhere today!|first=Nanci|last=Dagg|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=September 4, 2018}}</ref> The track was the lead single off his debut six-track extended play ''Shawn Austin'', released on September 28, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thereviewsarein.com/2018/09/29/shawn-austin-debut-album-review/|title=Shawn Austin, Debut Album Review|date=September 29, 2018|first=Joshua|last=Murray|work=The Reviews Are In}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2018/09/28/review-shawn-austin/|title=Review – Shawn Austin |first=Amanda|last=Hather|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=September 28, 2018}}</ref> In 2019, he released the single "What Do I Know",<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2019/04/18/shawn-austin-releases-new-single-what-do-i-know/|title=Shawn Austin releases new single, "What Do I Know"|first=Amanda|last=Hather|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=April 18, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://topcountry.ca/pick-shawn-austin-what-do-i-know/|title=Top Country Pick: Shawn Austin's What Do I Know|date=August 14, 2019|work=Top Country}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://thereviewsarein.com/2019/06/13/shawn-austin-what-do-i-know-lyric-video-release-5-quick-questions/|title=Shawn Austin – What Do I Know Lyric Video Release & 5 Quick Questions|date=June 13, 2019|first1=Joshua|last1=Murray|first2=Trish|last2=Cassling|work=The Reviews Are In}}</ref> which later peaked at number seven on Canada Country and became a new career high.<ref name="CCW"/> He followed that up with "The Little Things" in 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.completecountry.ca/article.php?article_id=5453|title=EXCLUSIVE PREMIERE: Shawn Austin's "The Little Things"|date=February 17, 2020|work=Complete Country}}</ref> That same year, he appeared on the [[NBC]] television show ''[[Songland]]'' in the United States, where he pitched a song titled "Ain't Goin' Nowhere" to [[Florida Georgia Line]], and later partnered with [[Ester Dean]] on reworking it with the title "Lean On".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://theboot.com/florida-georgia-line-songland/|title=Florida Georgia Line Help Make a Hit on 'Songland' [WATCH]|work=The Boot|access-date=April 8, 2023|date=May 18, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://meaww.com/songland-ester-dean-tweaks-shawn-austin-aint-goin-nowhere-to-lean-on-fans-love-florida-georgia-line|title='Songland': Ester transforms Shawn's original song into 'Lean On', wins over fans with appealing upgrade|work=Meaww|first=Sushma|last=Karra|date=May 18, 2020|access-date=April 8, 2023}}</ref> Ultimately, Austin finished as a runner-up, as Florida Georgia Line chose In August 2021, Austin signed with [[Big Loud Records]] and their Canadian-based imprint Local Hay,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2021/08/31/interview-shawn-austin/|title=Interview – Shawn Austin|first=Nanci|last=Dagg|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=August 31, 2021}}</ref> a joint venture between the label, [[Dallas Smith]], producer Scott Cooke, and partner Alex Seif.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://bigloudrecords.com/shawn-austin-signed-as-flagship-artist-to-big-loud-dallas-smith-joint-venture-local-hay/|title=SHAWN AUSTIN SIGNED AS FLAGSHIP ARTIST TO BIG LOUD / DALLAS SMITH JOINT VENTURE, LOCAL HAY|date=August 11, 2021|work=[[Big Loud Records]]}}</ref> He then released the single "[[Tailgate to Heaven]]" featuring American country singer [[Chris Lane]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/music/tailgate-to-heaven-shawn-austin-chris-lane/|title=Shawn Austin Talks About Single "Tailgate to Heaven" ft. Chris Lane|first=Jenna|last=Weishar|date=September 1, 2021|work=Front Porch Music}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Katie|last=Colley|title=Shawn Austin Teams Up With Country Superstar Chris Lane On New Single 'Tailgate To 11, 2021|work=[[Entertainment Tonight Canada|ET Canada]]|date=August 11, 2021}}</ref> Austin was an opening act on [[Dallas Smith]]'s "Some Things Never Change Tour" in the spring and early summer of 2022.<ref>{{cite web|title=Dallas Smith has announced The Some Things Never Change 2022 Tour and debuted his new single "Hide from a Broken Heart" at a special performance on CCCMA Awards Show|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2021/11/29/dallas-smith-has-announced-the-some-things-never-change-2022-tour-and-debuted-his-new-single-hide-from-a-broken-heart-at-a-special-performance-on-oh-cccma-awards-show/|first=Nanci|last=Dagg|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=November 29, 2021}}</ref> In June 2022, he released the extended play ''Planes Don't Wait'', which included "[[Tailgate to Heaven]]" and the singles "Get You" and "Slip".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.labeat.ca/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=6937:shawn-austin-getting-road-legs-back-on-some-things-never-change-tour|title=Shawn Austin getting road legs back on Some Things Never Change tour |first=Richard|last=Amery|work=The Lethbridge Alberta Beat|date=June 2, 2022|access-date=June 23, 2022}}</ref><ref name="ODT">{{cite web|url=https://519magazine.com/shawn-austin-returns-to-budweiser-gardens-tonight-with-old-dominion/|title=Shawn Austin Returns to Budweiser Gardens Tonight with Old Dominion|first=Whitney|last=South|work=519 Magazine|date=January 30, 2023|access-date=February 6, 2023}}</ref> In 2023, Austin was an opener for [[Old Dominion (band)|Old Dominion]] on the Canadian dates of their "No Bad Vibes Tour".<ref name="ODT"/> Later that year, he released the extended play ''Dirt Roads Downtown'', which includes the single "Settle for a Drink".<ref>{{cite web|url= https://canadianbeats.ca/2023/07/27/zoomies-shawn-austin-unveils-ep-dirt-roads-downtown/|title=ZOOMIES – SHAWN AUSTIN UNVEILS EP, DIRT ROADS DOWNTOWN|date=July 27, 2023|first=Jenna|last=Melanson|work=Canadian Beats Media|access-date=September 24, 2023}}</ref> In the spring of 2024, Austin embarked on "The Country Mixtape Tour" across Canada as a co-headliner alongside [[Tyler Joe Miller]] and [[Andrew Hyatt]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.soundcheckentertainment.ca/tyler-joe-miller-shawn-austin-and-andrew-hyatt-team-up-for-the-2024-country-mixtape-tour/|title=TYLER JOE MILLER, SHAWN AUSTIN AND ANDREW HYATT TEAM UP
