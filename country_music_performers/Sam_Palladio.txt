'''Sam Christian Palladio Scott''' (born 21 November 1986)<ref name=abcm>{{cite web|url=http://www.abcmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id%3D003527%26type%3Dpalladio |title=ABC Medianet |accessdate=6 October 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121014215105/http://www.abcmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=003527&type=palladio |archivedate=14 October 2012 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Hyman |first=Dan |url=http://www.elle.com/news/lifestyle/charles-esten-and-sam-palladio-nashville |title=Charles Esten and Sam Palladio of Nashville - Charles Esten and Sam Palladio Interview |website=Elle |date=2012-12-05 |accessdate=2016-06-16}}</ref> is an English actor and musician. He is best known for his starring role as [[Gunnar Scott]] in the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] musical drama series ''[[Nashville (2012 TV series)|Nashville]]'' (2012–18). Palladio has also had recurring roles on the comedy series ''[[Episodes (TV series)|Episodes]]'' (2012–15) and the science fiction series ''[[Humans (TV series)|Humans]]'' (2015–2018).<ref>[http://tvline.com/2012/08/14/fall-tv-preview-nashville-abc/ "Fall TV First Impression: ABC's Nashville Sings"], ''[[TVLine]]'', 14 August 2012</ref><ref>[http://www.tvfanatic.com/2012/08/fall-premiere-preview-spend-time-in-nashville/ "Fall Premiere Preview: Spend Time in Nashville"], TVfanatic, 9 August 2012</ref><ref>[http://omg.yahoo.com/video/clare-bowen-sam-palladio-howd-140000820.html "Clare Bowen And Sam Palladio: How'd They Get The Accent Right For ABC's 'Nashville'?"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120824170149/http://omg.yahoo.com/video/clare-bowen-sam-palladio-howd-140000820.html |date=24 August 2012 }}, [[Access Hollywood]], 22 August 2012</ref> His feature film credits include ''[[7 Lives]]'' (2011), ''[[Runner, Runner (film)|Runner, Runner]]'' (2013), ''[[Strange Magic (film)|Strange Magic]]'' (2015), ''[[The Princess Switch]]'' (2018), ''[[The Princess Switch: Switched Again]]'' (2020) and ''[[The Princess Switch 3: Romancing the Star]]'' (2021).

==Early life==
Palladio was born in [[Pembury]], [[Kent]], [[England]], [[UK]].<ref name="Betts">{{cite news |title=Sam Palladio, 'Nashville' Star, Makes Music City Memories |url=http://www.theboot.com/2013/02/21/sam-palladio-nashville |work=The Boot |date=21 February 2013 |accessdate=27 March 2013 |last=Betts |first=Stephen L.}}</ref> He was brought up by his parents, who are both artists, in [[Penzance]], [[Cornwall]], [[England]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://abc.go.com/shows/nashville/cast/gunnar-scott-character-bio |title=Sam Palladio as Gunnar Scott &#124; Nashville |website=Abc.go.com |accessdate=2016-06-16 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160609093813/http://abc.go.com/shows/nashville/cast/gunnar-scott-character-bio |archivedate=9 June 2016 |df=dmy-all }}</ref> Palladio was educated at [[Humphry Davy School]]<ref>{{Cite web |title=Category:Sam Palladio - Wikimedia Commons |url=https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Sam_Palladio |access-date=2022-05-24 |website=commons.wikimedia.org |language=en}}</ref> and then studied Actor Musicianship at [[Rose Bruford College]] in [[Sidcup]], [[London]], graduating in 2008.

==Career==
Palladio began his career with guest starring roles in various television series, including ''[[Little Crackers]]'', ''[[Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors]]'', ''[[The Hour (2011 TV series)|The Hour]]'', and ''[[Cardinal Burns]]''. He gained further recognition for his starring role as Gunnar Scott in the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] musical drama series ''[[Nashville (2012 TV series)|Nashville]]''. The series premiered to critical acclaim in 2012. It ended its run after six seasons in 2018. He also had a recurring role as Stoke on the comedy series ''[[Episodes (TV series)|Episodes]]'' between 2012 and 2015.<ref>[http://tvline.com/2012/08/14/fall-tv-preview-nashville-abc/ Fall TV First Impression: ABC's Nashville Sings], [[TVLine]], 14 August 2012</ref><ref>[http://www.tvfanatic.com/2012/08/fall-premiere-preview-spend-time-in-nashville/ Fall Premiere Preview: Spend Time In Nashville], TVfanatic, 9 August 2012</ref><ref>[http://omg.yahoo.com/video/clare-bowen-sam-palladio-howd-140000820.html Clare Bowen And Sam Palladio: How'd They Get The Accent Right For ABC's 'Nashville'?] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120824170149/http://omg.yahoo.com/video/clare-bowen-sam-palladio-howd-140000820.html |date=24 August 2012 }}, [[Access Hollywood]], 22 August 2012</ref> Since 2016, he has had a recurring role as Ed in the science fiction drama series ''[[Humans (TV series)|Humans]]''.

His film credits include roles as Calvin in the British drama film ''[[7 Lives]]'' (2011), as Shecky in the crime thriller film ''[[Runner, Runner (film)|Runner, Runner]]'' (2013), and as Roland in the animated musical fantasy film ''[[Strange Magic (film)|Strange Magic]]'' (2015). He was also the lead singer in a band called Salt Water Thief.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/saltwaterthief |title=Salt Water Thief &#124; Free Listening on SoundCloud |website=Soundcloud.com |date= |accessdate=2016-06-16}}</ref> In 2020, he appeared on an episode of ''[[The Repair Shop]]'', receiving his grandfather's [[RAF]] cap after it had been restored by the programme's experts.<ref>{{cite tweet|user=SamPalladio|number=1240329982590795782|title=Tune in to @TheRepairShop tonight as they repair something very special to my family and my Grandpa Owen...|date=18 March 2020}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Palladio has been in a relationship with American musician [[Cassadee Pope]] since December 2017.<ref>{{cite news|last=Mizoguchi|first=Karen|title=Cassadee Pope Talks 'Super Easy Breezy' Romance with Sam Palladio — and Their Paw-fect First Date|url=https://people.com/music/cassadee-pope-sam-palladio-super-easy-breezy-romance-dog-park-date/|access-date=October 6, 2018|publisher=People|date=March 29, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Carballo|first=Charlie|title=CMT Music Awards 2019 Celebrity Red Carpet Arrivals|url=https://footwearnews.com/gallery/2019-cmt-music-awards-celebrity-red-carpet-photos/|access-date=June 9, 2019|publisher=Footwear News|date=June 5, 2019}}</ref>
