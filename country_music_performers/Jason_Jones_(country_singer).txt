'''Jason Jones''' (born October 17, 1979 in [[Tallahassee, Florida]]) is an American [[country music]] singer. He is signed to [[Warner Bros. Records]] Nashville, and made his chart debut in May 2011 with the single "Ferris Wheel". The song is included on a six-song digital [[extended play]], released on May 31.

==Biography==
Jones said that he was introduced to country music while driving between Florida and Georgia. He formed a rock band in high school, and later dropped out of [[Florida State University]] to move to [[Nashville, Tennessee]] to begin his musical career. In Nashville, he joined a band which performed at the Wildhorse Saloon, and signed to a songwriting contract with Warner/Chappell.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tribstar.com/features/x1540196891/Jason-Jones-to-lift-audience-up-at-Mulligan-s|title=Jason Jones to lift audience up at Mulligan's|date=15 April 2011|work=Terre Haute Tribune-Star|accessdate=1 June 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/jason-jones-debut-single-ep-interview/|title=Jason Jones Follows His Heart Into Country Music With Debut Single and New EP|last=Conaway|first=Alanna|work=Taste of Country|accessdate=1 June 2011}}</ref>

Jones was slated to be working with songwriter and producer [[Brett Beavers]] on his debut album, which would have been titled ''Ferris Wheel''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.warnermusicnashville.com/artist/jason-jones |title=Biography |work=Warner Music Nashville |accessdate=1 June 2011 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726112546/http://www.warnermusicnashville.com/artist/jason-jones |archivedate=26 July 2011 }}</ref> The album's title track was released in May 2011, and it entered the [[Hot Country Songs]] charts at number 59 for the chart dated June 11, 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/billboardbiz/photos/pdf/country_update_0531.pdf|title=Hot Country Songs|date=31 May 2011|work=Billboard Country Update|page=10|accessdate=1 June 2011}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Jones resides in Tennessee. He is married and he has three sons.
