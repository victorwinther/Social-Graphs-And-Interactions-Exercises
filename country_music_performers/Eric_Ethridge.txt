'''Eric Ethridge''' is a Canadian [[country pop]] singer and songwriter. He has released two albums ''[[Good with Me]]'' (2020) and ''Family First'' (2024), and has two gold-certified singles in Canada with "[[California (Wave song)|California]]" and "[[If You Met Me First]]".<ref>{{cite news|title=Sarnia's Eric Ethridge is on the road again|url=https://www.theobserver.ca/entertainment/local-arts/sarnias-eric-ethridge-is-on-the-road-again|first=Paul|last=Morden|work=The Observer|publisher=[[Postmedia Network]]|date=September 1, 2020|access-date=December 27, 2020}}</ref><ref name="CBM"/><ref>{{cite certification|region=Canada|artist=Eric Ethridge|access-date=August 1, 2023}}</ref>

==Early life==
Eric Ethridge was raised in [[Sarnia]], [[Ontario]] and played [[Canadian football|football]] as a [[defensive end]] at the [[University of Western Ontario]].<ref name="LFP">{{cite news|title=Country Music Awards: Sarnia's Eric Ethridge wins Discovery Award|url=https://lfpress.com/2016/09/10/country-music-awards-sarnias-eric-ethridge-wins-discovery-award|work=The Observer|publisher=[[Postmedia Network]]|date=September 10, 2016|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref> Prior to pursuing a full-time career in music, Ethridge attended a chiropractic school in [[Toronto]] and worked as a chiropractor in Sarnia.<ref name="Observer">{{cite news|title=Sarnia's Eric Ethridge signs record deal|url=https://www.theobserver.ca/entertainment/local-arts/sarnias-eric-ethridge-signs-record-deal|first=Paul|last=Morden|work=The Observer|publisher=[[Postmedia Network]]|date=November 30, 2018|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref>

==Career==
===Early career and self-titled EP===
In 2016, he won the [[Canadian Country Music Association]]'s Discovery Award.<ref name="Observer"/>

In July 2018, he released his self-titled debut EP ''Eric Ethridge''.<ref>{{cite news|title=Eric Ethridge enjoying a 'slow burn'|url=https://www.theobserver.ca/news/local-news/eric-ethridge-enjoying-a-slow-burn|first=Paul|last=Morden|work=The Sarnia Observer|publisher=[[Postmedia Network]]|date=July 6, 2018|access-date=December 29, 2020}}</ref> The single "Liquor's Callin' the Shots" reached the top 30 of Canadian country radio.<ref name="Observer"/> In August 2018, he won the Grand Prize in the 2018 Unsigned Only Music Competition for his song "Girl On Fire".<ref>{{cite web |title=Canadian Country Artist Eric Ethridge Wins Prestigious Grand Prize in 2018 Unsigned Only Music Competition |url=https://www.prweb.com/releases/canadian_country_artist_eric_ethridge_wins_prestigious_grand_prize_in_2018_unsigned_only_music_competition/prweb15706776.htm |website=PR Web |date=August 23, 2018|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref>

In November 2018, Ethridge signed a worldwide recording and publishing deal with ole, who placed him on their label, Red Dot.<ref>{{cite news|title=ole Signs Singer-Songwriter Eric Ethridge to Worldwide Recording and Publishing Deal|url=https://feldman-agency.com/post-216/ole-signs-singer-songwriter-eric-ethridge-to-worldwide-reco|work=The Feldman Agency|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref> Further singles from the EP included "Makin' Me Crazy",<ref name="Observer"/> "[[California (Wave song)|California]]",<ref>{{cite web|url=http://frontporchmusic.ca/music/eric-ethridge-covers-california/|title=Eric Ethridge Covers "California" in New Single|last=Jenna|date=October 25, 2018|website=Front Porch Music|language=en-US|access-date=April 22, 2019}}</ref> and "[[If You Met Me First]]",<ref>{{cite magazine|title=Eric Ethridge Pines For What Could Have Been in 'If You Met Me First' Video: Exclusive|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/country/8510744/eric-ethridge-if-you-met-me-first-video|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|first=Annie|last=Reuter|date=May 9, 2019}}</ref> the latter two of which were certified Gold by [[Music Canada]].<ref name="Gold">{{cite web|first=Brent|last=Lale|title=Local musician Eric Ethridge has two records reach 'gold' status|url=https://london.ctvnews.ca/local-musician-eric-ethridge-has-two-records-reach-gold-status-1.5250733|work=[[CTV News]] London|date=January 1, 2021}}</ref>

===2020-present: ''Good with Me''===
In February 2020, Ethridge released his debut US single "[[Dream Girl (Eric Ethridge song)|Dream Girl]]", co-written by [[Dan + Shay]].<ref>{{cite news|title=ERIC ETHRIDGE HEADS TO THE U.S. WITH NEW SINGLE, 'DREAM GIRL'|first=Drew|last=Pearce|url=https://www.soundslikenashville.com/music/eric-ethridge-dream-girl/|work=Sounds Like Nashville|date=December 28, 2020|access-date=December 10, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Eric Ethridge's 'Dream Girl' Becomes a Precious Memory in Dan + Shay-Penned Single [Exclusive Premiere]|first=Cillea|last=Houghton|url=https://tasteofcountry.com/eric-ethridges-dream-girl-dan-and-shay/|work=Taste of Country|date=February 20, 2020|access-date=January 5, 2021}}</ref> The track was then included on his April 2020 extended play ''Forever With You''.<ref>{{cite web|first=Alyssa|last=Croezen|title=Eric Ethridge Debuts 'Dream Girl' Music Video, Drops New EP 'Forever With You'|url=https://etcanada.com/news/631187/eric-ethridge-debuts-dream-girl-music-video-drops-new-ep-forever-with-you/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200428142932/https://etcanada.com/news/631187/eric-ethridge-debuts-dream-girl-music-video-drops-new-ep-forever-with-you/|url-status=dead|archive-date=April 28, 2020|work=[[Entertainment Tonight Canada|ET Canada]]|date=April 24, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Sarnia's Eric Ethridge is off the road but still making music|url=https://www.theobserver.ca/entertainment/local-arts/sarnias-eric-ethridge-is-off-the-road-but-still-making-music|first=Paul|last=Morden|work=The Sarnia Observer|publisher=[[Postmedia Network]]|date=April 17, 2020|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref>

In August 2020, Ethridge released the single "Kiss Me Goodbye"<ref>{{Cite web|first=Nanci|last=Dagg|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2020/08/28/eric-ethridge-releases-his-new-single-kiss-me-goodbye/|title=Eric Ethridge releases his new single, "Kiss Me Goodbye"|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=August 28, 2020|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref> On October 30, 2020, Ethridge released his debut full-length album ''[[Good with Me]]'' on [[Anthem Records]] (rebranded from Red Dot),<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.iheartradio.ca/purecountry/kingston/audio/eric-ethridge-and-his-new-album-good-with-me-1.13838751|title=Eric Ethridge and his NEW album 'Good with Me'|work=Pure Country 99|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|first=Kim|last=Hughes|url=https://www.partonandpearl.com/blog/album-review-eric-ethridge-good-with-me|title=Album Review: Eric Ethridge, Good with Me|work=Parton and Pearl|date=November 5, 2020|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref> which includes
the singles "[[Dream Girl (Eric Ethridge song)|Dream Girl]]", "Kiss Me Goodbye",<ref name="CBM">{{Cite web|first=Nanci|last=Dagg|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2020/11/03/november-is-starting-off-to-be-a-great-one-for-eric-ethridge-with-the-release-of-his-new-album-good-with-me-and-his-current-single-kiss-me-goodbye-still-climbing-the-charts/|title=November is starting off to be a great one for Eric Ethridge with the release of his new album Good with Me and his current single "Kiss Me Goodbye" still climbing the charts|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=November 3, 2020|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.anthementertainment.com/news/good-with-me-the-new-album-from-ccma-award-nominated-country-artist-eric-ethridge-available-today-10-30/|title=ALBUM FROM CCMA AWARD NOMINATED COUNTRY ARTIST ERIC ETHRIDGE AVAILABLE TODAY (10/30)|work=[[Anthem Records|Anthem Entertainment]]|date=October 30, 2020|access-date=January 3, 2021}}</ref> and "Sad Songs".<ref>{{Cite web|first=Nanci|last=Dagg|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2021/02/12/listen-to-eric-ethridges-new-single-sad-songs-to-be-captivated-by-the-distinctive-insight-it-delivers/|title=Listen to Eric Ethridge's new single "Sad Songs" to be captivated by the distinctive insight it delivers|work=Canadian Beats Media|date=February 12, 2021|access-date=February 14, 2021}}</ref>

In 2022, Ethridge signed a management deal with Workshop Music Group and independently released the single "Made in Mexico".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://frontporchmusic.ca/music/eric-ethridge-made-in-mexico/|title=Eric Ethridge Releases New Single "Made In Mexico" And Signs Management Deal With Workshop Music Group|first=Jenna|last=Weishar|date=October 5, 2022|work=Front Porch Music|access-date=February 6, 2023}}</ref>
