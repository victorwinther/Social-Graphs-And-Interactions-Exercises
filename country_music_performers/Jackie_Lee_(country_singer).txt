'''Jackie Lee''' (born October 2, 1991 in [[Maryville, Tennessee]]<ref name="nashville">{{cite news|last1=Holthouse|first1=Jerry|title=EXCLUSIVE INTERVIEW: Rising Star, Jackie Lee|url=http://www.nashville.com/music/2014/11/exclusive-interview-rising-star-jackie-lee/|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=Nashville.com|date=November 5, 2014|archive-date=November 10, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141110004122/http://www.nashville.com/music/2014/11/exclusive-interview-rising-star-jackie-lee/|url-status=dead}}</ref>) is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter.

==Career==
Before pursuing a career in music, Lee played high school football at Alcoa High, where he was a teammate of future NFL receiver [[Randall Cobb (American football)|Randall Cobb]].<ref name="dailytimes">Kendra Meinert, "[http://www.thedailytimes.com/news/cobb-s-old-alcoa-football-pal-lands-lambeau-anthem-gig/article_69df2c3f-4739-5278-9850-14dae61fc02e.html Cobb's Old Alcoa Football Pal Lands Lambeau Anthem Gig]," ''Maryville-Alcoa (TN) Daily Times'', December 25, 2014.</ref><ref name="musicrow">{{cite news|last1=Nicholson|first1=Jessica|title=MusicRowPics: Jackie Lee|url=http://www.musicrow.com/2014/11/musicrowpics-jackie-lee/|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=[[MusicRow]]|date=November 19, 2014}}</ref> He grew up singing in church and his dad was a professional musician.<ref name="nashville" /> Lee was briefly signed to [[Republic Nashville]] in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bigmachinelabelgroup.com/jackie-lee-inks-deal-with-republic-nashville/|title=JACKIE LEE INKS DEAL WITH REPUBLIC NASHVILLE|date=February 16, 2012|work=Big Machine Records|accessdate=May 29, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305204717/http://www.bigmachinelabelgroup.com/jackie-lee-inks-deal-with-republic-nashville/|archive-date=March 5, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>

He signed to [[Broken Bow Records]] and released his debut single, "She Does", in September 2014.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bjorke|first1=Matt|title=New Artist Spotlight: Jackie Lee|url=http://roughstock.com/news/2014/11/30606-new-artist-spotlight-jackie-lee/|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=Roughstock|date=November 7, 2014}}</ref> The song was written by [[Kelley Lovelace]] and [[Neil Thrasher]] and originally put on hold by [[Kenny Chesney]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Ward|first1=Scott|title=New Artist Jackie Lee's "She Does" is an Impassioned Introduction to County Fans – New in Nashville|url=http://kfrog.cbslocal.com/2014/09/15/new-artist-jackie-lees-she-does-is-an-impassioned-introduction-to-county-fans-new-in-nashville/|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=[[KFRG]]|date=September 15, 2014|archive-date=December 13, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213015140/http://kfrog.cbslocal.com/2014/09/15/new-artist-jackie-lees-she-does-is-an-impassioned-introduction-to-county-fans-new-in-nashville/|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Freeman|first1=Jon|title=Jackie Lee Premieres Lyric Video for "She Does"|url=http://www.countryweekly.com/videos/jackie-lee-premieres-lyric-video-she-does|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=[[Country Weekly]]|date=November 26, 2014}}</ref>

Markos Papadatos of ''Digital Journal'' gave the song four stars out of five, writing that "Lee's vocals on 'She Does' are rich, sultry and polished, where the listener can easily recall country singer-songwriters [[Will Hoge]] and [[James Otto]]."<ref>{{cite news|last1=Papadatos|first1=Markos|title=Review: Jackie Lee impresses on debut country single 'She Does'|url=http://www.digitaljournal.com/a-and-e/music/review-jackie-lee-impresses-on-debut-country-single-she-does/article/403868|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=Digital Journal|date=September 17, 2014}}</ref> The song also received a favorable review from ''[[Taste of Country]]'', which called it "a country love song that stands out from so many others on the radio" and said that "Lee is a pure country vocalist who will attract both fans of traditional country and fans of a slicker sound."<ref>{{cite news|title=Jackie Lee, 'She Does' [Listen]|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/jackie-lee-she-does/|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=[[Taste of Country]]|date=November 20, 2014}}</ref> "She Does" was the most added song at country radio the week of September 15, 2014.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Harr|first1=Dan|title=Jackie Lee's Debut Single "She Does" Is #1 Most Added Song at Country Radio|url=http://www.musicnewsnashville.com/jackie-lees-debut-single-1-added-song-country-radio/|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=Music News Nashville|date=September 16, 2014|archive-date=December 13, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213083147/http://www.musicnewsnashville.com/jackie-lees-debut-single-1-added-song-country-radio/|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Papadatos|first1=Markos|title=Interview: Jackie Lee talks country single 'She Does' and career|url=http://www.digitaljournal.com/a-and-e/music/interview-jackie-lee-talks-country-single-she-does-and-career/article/406049|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=Digital Journal|date=September 29, 2014}}</ref> It debuted at number 57 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Country Airplay]] chart for the week of October 4.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Morris|first1=Edward|title=Tim McGraw Tops Album Chart With ''Sundown Heaven Town''|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/1731317/tim-mcgraw-tops-album-chart-with-sundown-heaven-town/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141201184727/http://www.cmt.com/news/1731317/tim-mcgraw-tops-album-chart-with-sundown-heaven-town/|url-status=dead|archive-date=December 1, 2014|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=[[Country Music Television]]|date=September 26, 2014}}</ref>

Lee made his ''[[Grand Ole Opry]]'' debut in September 2014.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Dauphin|first1=Chuck|title=Jackie Lee Scores a Touchdown With First Single, 'She Does,' Prepping Debut Album|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-615/6289425/jackie-lee-she-does-broken-bown-records-debut-album|accessdate=December 11, 2014|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=October 22, 2014}}</ref> Lee's second single, "Headphones", was released to country radio on May 11, 2015. Lee released his third single, "Leave the Light On", through Spotify on July 8, 2016. A fourth single, "Getting Over You", was released to country radio in 2016. Lee parted ways with Broken Bow in July 2017.
