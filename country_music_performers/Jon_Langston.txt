'''Jonathan Thomas Langston''' (born April 8, 1991) is an American [[country music]]ian from [[Loganville, Georgia]]. He released an [[extended play]], ''Showtime'', with Treehouse Records, in 2015. This was his breakthrough release upon the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] charts. His extended play, ''Jon Langston'', was released independently in 2015. The release got better placements upon the same ''Billboard'' magazine charts.

==Music career==

His music career began at a show in [[Kennesaw, Georgia]] in 2013, where he was the opening act for [[Chase Rice]].<ref name="DSM">{{cite web|first=Jackie |last=Gutknecht |title=Southern Sound: Jon Langston headed for music city |url=http://deepsouthmag.com/2014/06/southern-sound-jon-langston-headed-for-music-city/ |publisher=Deep South Magazine |accessdate=December 23, 2015 |date=June 19, 2014}}</ref> He released an independently made [[extended play]] in 2013, ''Runnin{{'}} on Sunshine''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Runnin' on Sunshine EP by Jon Langston|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/runnin-on-sunshine-ep/id683306472|publisher=[[iTunes]]|accessdate=December 23, 2015}}</ref> The [[extended play]], ''Showtime'', was released on May 5, 2015, with Treehouse Records.<ref>{{cite web|title=Showtime EP by Jon Langston|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/showtime-ep/id986961345|publisher=[[iTunes]]|accessdate=December 23, 2015}}</ref> This extended play was his breakthrough release upon the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] charts, where it peaked at No. 26 on the [[Top Country Albums]] chart, No. 22 on the [[Independent Albums]] chart, and No. 8 on the [[Top Heatseekers|Heatseekers Albums]] chart.<ref name="chart search">[http://www.billboard.com/biz/search/charts?f%5b0%5d=ts_chart_artistname%3AJon%20Langston&f%5b1%5d=ss_bb_type%3Achart_item&type=2&artist=Jon%20Langston Chart search for Jon Langston] at billboard.com/biz</ref> His second extended play, ''Jon Langston'', was independently released on December 11, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|title=Jon Langston – EP by Jon Langston|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/jon-langston-ep/id1066003418|publisher=[[iTunes]]|accessdate=December 23, 2015}}</ref> It placed on the same ''Billboard'' magazine charts, where this time it got No. 25 on the Top Country Albums, No. 13 on Independent Albums, and No. 1 on the Heatseekers Albums.<ref name="chart search"/>

Langston is featured on the song "Yung and Dum" by American metalcore band [[Issues (band)|Issues]], taken from their second full-length album, ''[[Headspace (Issues album)|Headspace]]'', which was released on May 20, 2016. In February 2018 it was announced on his Instagram page that he would be signing a record deal with [[UMG Nashville]].

In February 2018, Langston released the single "When It Comes To Loving You" which received considerable attention,<ref>{{cite web |url=http://headlineplanet.com/home/2018/02/10/jon-langstons-comes-loving-reaches-1-us-itunes-sales-chart/ |title=Jon Langston's "When It Comes To Loving You" Reaches #1 On US iTunes Sales Chart|first= Brian|last= Cantor |work=Headline Planet }}</ref> and it became the second best-selling country song of the week with 21,000 copies sold.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://roughstock.com/news/2018/02/42804-top-30-country-digital-singles-february-19-2018|title=Top 30 Country Digital Singles: February 19, 2018|work=Roughstock|last=Bjorke|first=Matt|date=February 19, 2018|accessdate=March 12, 2018}}</ref> It reached No. 29 on Hot Country Songs, his second single to appear on the chart after  "Right Girl Wrong Time" in 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8213433/kelsea-ballerini-fourth-country-airplay-no-1-legends |title=Kelsea Ballerini Collects Fourth Country Airplay No. 1 With 'Legends' |date=February 21, 2018 |first= Jim |last=Asker |work=Billboard }}</ref> In August 2018 Jon was assigned to UMG Nashville imprint EMI Records Nashville and 32 Bridge Entertainment, an imprint founded by [[Luke Bryan]]. Langston made his debut at the Grand Ole Opry on February 22, 2022.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.opry.com/artists/jon-langston-2/ |title=Jon Langston |date= March 1, 2022 }}</ref> He released the single "Back Words" in 2022.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.jonlangston.com/listen-now-jon-langston-drops-new-single-back-words/ |title=LISTEN NOW: JON LANGSTON DROPS NEW SINGLE "BACK WORDS" |date=February 25, 2022 }}</ref>
