'''John David Kent''' is a [[Country music|country]] band based out of [[Celeste, Texas|Celeste]], [[Texas|TX]]. Consisting of John David Kent, also known as John Kent, formerly of the late 1990s post-grunge band [[Radish (band)|Radish]], and other [[North Texas]] natives Randall Fuller, Tony Kent, and Colton Gilbreath.

== Background information==
After John's stint recording and touring with Radish, [[Ben Kweller]], and [[The Lemonheads]], he relocated back to his hometown of Celeste, TX to form [[Blackland Records]] and open "The Vault" Recording Studio.<ref>[http://www.dallasobserver.com/2010-12-02/music/john-david-kent-and-the-dumb-angels-letting-the-true-artist-shine-through/ Dallas Observer: 12/02/2010]</ref> John began to write songs of his own and formed the band Pony League which is now defunct but then put together John David Kent. The band's self-titled first album, released on May 17, 2011, is available on iTunes. Their second album was released in 2013.<ref>[http://npgpr.com/client1.php?id=3&page=3 NPG Public Relations<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110823105553/http://npgpr.com/client1.php?id=3&page=3 |date=2011-08-23 }}</ref>
