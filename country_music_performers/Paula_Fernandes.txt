'''Paula Fernandes de Souza'''<ref name="fullnameandbio">{{cite web|url=http://revistatpm.uol.com.br/revista/107/perfil/o-reinado-de-paula-fernandes.html|title=O reinado de Paula Fernandes |author=Ariane Abdallah|date=March 9, 2011|accessdate=January 1, 2012|publisher=Revista TPM|language=Portuguese}}</ref> ({{IPA|pt-BR|ˈpawlɐ feʁˈnɐ̃dʒis|lang}}; born August 28, 1984, in [[Sete Lagoas]], [[Minas Gerais]])<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dicionariompb.com.br/paula-fernandes/biografia|title=Paula Fernandes – Dicionário Cravo Albin de Música Brasileira |accessdate=August 1, 2011|publisher=Dicionário Cravo Albin da Música Popular Brasileira|language=Portuguese}}</ref> is a Brazilian singer-songwriter. She won [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Sertaneja Music Album|Latin Grammy Awards]] for her albums ''Amanhecer'' in 2016 and for ''Origens (Ao Vivo em Sete Lagoas)'' in 2020.

== Biography ==
[[File:Paula Fernandes, Angra dos Reis, Junho.2022 - 14.jpg|upright|thumb|left|Fernandes performing in 2022]]
Fernandes was born in Sete Lagoas, Minas Gerais, where she grew up on a farm with her parents and a younger brother.<ref name="fullnameandbio" /> She went to college to study Geography in [[Belo Horizonte]] when she was 18 years old.<ref name="correiobraziliense">{{cite web|url=http://www.correioweb.com.br/euestudante/noticias.php?id=16605|title=Sucesso em trilhas de novelas, Paula Fernandes raised popularity after sharing stage with the well known Brazilian singer Roberto Carlos|date=January 5, 2011|accessdate=March 29, 2011|publisher=[[Correio Braziliense]]|language=Portuguese}}</ref>

Fernandes started singing when she was eight years old, and released a self-titled debut album (''Paula Fernandes'') in 1993.<ref name="contigo">{{cite web|url=http://contigo.abril.com.br/famosos/paula-fernandes/cantora/vida|title=Paula Fernandes|accessdate=March 29, 2011|publisher=Contigo|language=Portuguese|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110503073420/http://contigo.abril.com.br/famosos/paula-fernandes/cantora/vida|archivedate=May 3, 2011}}</ref> Her second album, ''Ana Rayo'', was released in 1995, inspired by the [[telenovela]] ''A História de Ana Raio e Zé Trovão''.<ref name="contigo" />  In 2005, she recorded "Ave Maria Natureza", one of the theme songs of [[Rede Globo]]'s telenovela ''[[América (telenovela)|América]]''. She released the album ''Canções do Vento Sul'' in 2005,<ref name="contigo" /> for which she was nominated for the Best Popular Singer Award at the Prêmio da Música Brasileira.<ref name="correiobraziliense" /> In 2007, Fernandes released an album in English titled ''Dust in the Wind'', in an American western style.

The song "Jeito de Mato" from her 2009 album, ''Pássaro de Fogo'', was used as a theme song on the telenovela ''Paraíso'', and her version of [[Ivete Sangalo]]'s "Quando a Chuva Passar" was the main theme of the telenovela ''Escrito nas Estrelas''.<ref name="contigo" /> Fernandes released her first DVD in October 2010,<ref name="contigo" /> and gained further recognition after participating in two Rede Globo specials: the [[Roberto Carlos (singer)|Roberto Carlos]]' end of year show, broadcast on December 25, 2010, and ''Show da Virada'', broadcast on New Year's Eve, 2010.<ref name="contigo" /> Her live album, ''Paula Fernandes – Ao Vivo'', released in 2011, was the top seller that year. Fernandes became known across [[Latin America]] with her collaboration on the song "[[Hoy Me Voy]]", which was released on [[Juanes]]' 2012 album ''[[Juanes MTV Unplugged|MTV Unplugged]]''.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://entretenimento.r7.com/musica/noticias/paula-fernandes-coloca-disco-ao-vivo-no-topo-da-lista-de-mais-vendidos-20110314.html|title=Paula Fernandes coloca disco ao vivo no topo da lista de mais vendidos|author=R7|publisher=Rede Record|accessdate=March 29, 2011|language=Portuguese}}</ref>

Fernandes' 2013 album ''Multishow ao Vivo – Um Ser Amor'' was nominated for the 2014 [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Sertaneja Music Album]], with the title song being nominated in the [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Brazilian Song|Best Brazilian Song]] category.<ref name=LatinGrammy2014>{{cite web|first1=Andrea|last1=Wang|first2=Tracy|last2=Brown|title=Latin Grammys 2014: Complete list of nominees|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/envelope/music/la-et-ms-latin-grammys-2014-nominations-winners-list-20140924-story.html#page=2|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=September 29, 2014|date=September 24, 2014}}</ref> Fernandes' album ''Hora Certa'' was nominated for a Latin Grammy Award in 2019,<ref name="billboardLG19">{{cite magazine |last1=Cabo |first1=Leila |title=Latin Grammys 2019 Winners: Complete List |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8543699/latin-grammys-2019-winners-list |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |publisher=[[MRC (company)|MRC]] |accessdate=September 9, 2020 |date=November 14, 2019}}</ref> and she won a second Latin Grammy award in 2020 for her album ''Origens (Ao Vivo em Sete Lagoas)'', which was recorded live in June 2019.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.setedias.com.br/noticia/destaques/53/paula-fernandes-concorre-ao-grammy-latino-2020-com-album-gravado-ao-vivo-em-sete-lagoas/23026 | title=Paula Fernandes concorre ao Grammy Latino 2020 com álbum gravado ao vivo em Sete Lagoas }}</ref>

Fernandes has sung a number of duets, and ensembles, with other artists including [[Michael Bolton]], [[Zezé Di Camargo & Luciano]],  [[Shania Twain]] and [[Taylor Swift]]. Fernandes has toured internationally, performing to more than 500,000 people in Italy, United Kingdom, Switzerland, Brazil, Portugal, United States, Luxembourg, Angola, Cape Verde, Spain, Argentina and Paraguay.<ref>{{Cite web |date=March 3, 2016 |title=www.tudosobresertanejo.com |url=http://www.tudosobresertanejo.com/2012/10/paula-fernandes-fala-sobre-turne-na.html//?ref=22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303165401/http://www.tudosobresertanejo.com/2012/10/paula-fernandes-fala-sobre-turne-na.html//?ref=22 |archive-date=March 3, 2016 |access-date=November 14, 2022 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=February 10, 2013 |title=Paula Fernandes fará turnê pelos Estados Unidos em fevereiro – Notícias – Contigo! |url=http://contigo.abril.com.br/noticias/sertanejo/paula-fernandes-fara-turne-pelos-estados-unidos-em-fevereiro |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130210151553/http://contigo.abril.com.br/noticias/sertanejo/paula-fernandes-fara-turne-pelos-estados-unidos-em-fevereiro |archive-date=February 10, 2013 |access-date=November 14, 2022 }}</ref>
