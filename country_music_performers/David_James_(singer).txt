'''David James''' is a Canadian [[country music]] singer-songwriter. James was nominated for the 2014 [[Canadian Country Music Association]] Discovery Award and 2017 Rising Star Award.<ref>{{cite news|title=Media Release: Top Six Finalists Announced For The 2014 CCMA Discovery Program|url=http://www.ccma.org/cgi/page.cgi/_article.html/News/Media_Release_Top_Six_Finalists_Announced_for_the_2014_CCMA_Discovery_Program-2014-06-11|accessdate=February 19, 2016|work=[[Canadian Country Music Association]]|date=April 30, 2014|archive-date=March 2, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160302194551/http://www.ccma.org/cgi/page.cgi/_article.html/News/Media_Release_Top_Six_Finalists_Announced_for_the_2014_CCMA_Discovery_Program-2014-06-11|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Career==

2014|archive-date=March 3, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303020057/http://www.musicnewsnashville.com/mdm-recordings-inc-signs-ccma-nominee-david-james/|url-status=dead}}</ref> His first single for the label, "What We Weren't Looking For", was released in February 2015.<ref name="cmr">{{cite news|title=David James Releases New Single "What We Weren't Looking channel)|CMT]] in March.<ref name="cmr" /> The song peaked at number 27 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Canada Country]] chart in June.<ref name="Canada Country" /> James' second single for MDM, "Some Hearts", was released in September.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Melanson|first1=Jenna|title=New single released by David James|url=http://canadianbeats.ca/2015/09/29/new-single-released-by-david-james/|accessdate=February 19, 2016|work=Canadian Beats|date=September 29, 2015}}</ref> His third single for the label, play]], ''Songs About a Girl'', released on March 11.<ref name="beats" /> In 2020, James released the extended play ''[[If I Were You (EP)|If I Were You]]'', which included the singles "Cars, Girls,
