'''Randy Schlappi''' (born September 23, 1985), known professionally as '''Randy Montana''', is an American [[country music|country]] singer signed to [[Mercury Records Nashville]]. His first album, ''Randy Montana'', was released in 2011.

==Early and personal life==
Randy Schlappi is the son of singer-songwriter [[Billy Montana]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://blog.gactv.com/blog/2010/02/25/sugarland-david-nail-josh-turner-a-massive-demonstration-at-the-ryman/ |title=Sugarland, David Nail, Josh Turner: a Massive Demonstration at the Ryman |author=Tom Roland |date=February 25, 2010 |work=[[Great American Country]] |accessdate=March 23, 2010}}</ref>

He is married to Montgomery "Gummy" Lee, daughter of retired [[stock car racing|stock car]] racer [[Kyle Petty]], and granddaughter of [[Richard Petty]]. The couple have one daughter Sullivan Mae Montana born February 27, 2012, and one son Gunnar West Montana born December 10, 2014.

==Musical career==
In March 2010, Montana released his debut single "Ain't Much Left of Lovin' You," to radio. The song entered at No.&nbsp;59 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Country Songs]] charts dated for the week ending April 3, 2010. His self-titled album's second single "1,000 Faces" released to country radio on 2011.

Karlie Justus of Engine 145 gave "Ain't Much Left of Lovin' You" a thumbs-up, calling Montana's voice "raspy" and comparing the song's theme to [[George Jones]]'s "[[The Grand Tour (song)|The Grand Tour]]."<ref name="engine145">{{cite web |url=http://www.engine145.com/randy-montana-aint-much-left-of-lovin-you/ |title=Randy Montana — "Ain't Much Left of Lovin' You" |author=Karlie Justus |date=March 18, 2010 |work=Engine 145 |accessdate=March 23, 2010 |archive-date=December 18, 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141218080820/http://www.engine145.com/randy-montana-aint-much-left-of-lovin-you/ |url-status=dead }}</ref> Matt Bjorke of Roughstock rated it three-and-a-half stars out of five, saying that the song is a "grow-on-you type" but that it "really feels like a genuine and proper introduction."<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.roughstock.com/blog/randy-montana-ain-t-much-left-of-lovin-you- |title=Randy Montana — "Ain't Much Left of Lovin' You" |author=Matt Bjorke |date=March 23, 2010 |work=Roughstock |accessdate=March 23, 2010 |archive-date=June 13, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110613154347/http://www.roughstock.com/blog/randy-montana-ain-t-much-left-of-lovin-you- |url-status=dead }}</ref>
