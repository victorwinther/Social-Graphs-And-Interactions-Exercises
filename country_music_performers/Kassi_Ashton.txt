'''Kassi Ashton''' (born '''Kassi Meisenheimer'''; February 3, 1994) is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[California, Missouri]] signed to [[Universal Music Group Nashville|MCA Nashville]] and [[Interscope Records]].<ref name="kassi ashton">{{cite web|url=https://www.kassiashton.com|title=Kassi Ashton Official Website|accessdate=December 29, 2023}}</ref>

==Background==
Kassi Ashton signed a joint recording deal with UMG Nashville and Interscope in December 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2017/12/kassi-ashton-inks-joint-deal-with-interscopeumg-nashville/|title=Kassi Ashton Inks Joint Deal With Interscope/UMG Nashville|author=Lorie Hollabaugh|date=December 14, 2017|publisher=Music Row|accessdate=December 29, 2023}}</ref> Her first promotional single with the MCA Nashville imprint was named after her hometown ("California, Missouri") and released in January 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tasteofcountry.com/kassi-ashton-california-missouri-song-interview/|title=Kassi Ashton's on California, Missouri: It's Complicated|author=Billy Dukes|date=January 19, 2018|publisher=Taste of Country|accessdate=December 29, 2023}}</ref> The label issued several promotional singles over the next four years, until "Dates in Pickup Trucks", which was serviced to country radio on February 28, 2022, as Ashton's debut single.<ref name="kassi ashton"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://people.com/country/kassi-ashton-song-dates-in-pickup-trucks-inspired-by-grandma/|title=Kassi Ashton on Why Love Is 'Worth It' — and How Her Grandma Inspired Her Song 'Dates in Pickup Trucks'|author=Tricia Despres|date=February 25, 2022|publisher=People Magazine|accessdate=December 29, 2023}}</ref> It reached a peak of number 57 on the ''Billboard'' [[Country Airplay]] chart. On March 13, 2023, "Drive You Out of My Mind" was released as her second single, which peaked at number 42 on the ''Billboard'' Country Airplay chart in September. Ashton made her debut on the [[Grand Ole Opry]] on March 15, 2023.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.instagram.com/p/Cp26gmdOP_f/?img_index=1 | title=Instagram }}</ref> 

"[[Called Crazy]]" debuted at number 53 on ''Billboard'' Country Airplay,<ref name="BCU">{{cite magazine|url=http://bulletins.billboard.com/country/country_update_2024_0329.pdf|title=Billboard Country Update|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=March 29, 2024|access-date=March 30, 2024}}</ref> Ashton was chosen as [[Audacy, Inc.]]'s 'Launch' Artist for her third single "Called Crazy",<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.audacy.com/national/music/meet-audacy-launch-artist-kassi-ashton|title=Meet Audacy 'Launch' artist, Kassi Ashton|author=Monica Rivera|date=May 6, 2024|website=Audacy|accessdate=May 6, 2024}}</ref> which became her first top 40 hit on the ''Billboard'' Country Airplay chart. Ashton's debut studio album, ''Made from the Dirt'', was released on September 20, 2024.<ref name="Debut Album"/>
