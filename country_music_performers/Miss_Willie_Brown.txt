'''Miss Willie Brown''' was an American [[country music]] duo consisting of Amanda Watkins and Kasey Buckley, who are respectively natives of [[West Virginia]] and [[Texas]] but met in [[California]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://site.amoctone.com/misswilliebrown |title=A&M Octone Records Miss Willie Brown : Artists |publisher=Site.amoctone.com |date= |accessdate=2012-07-26}}</ref> They are signed to [[A&M/Octone Records]], to which they were the first group signed.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.americansongwriter.com/2011/05/miss-willie-brown/ |title=Miss Willie Brown |publisher=American Songwriter |date=2011-05-06 |accessdate=2012-07-26}}</ref>

The duo performed on ''[[Jimmy Kimmel Live!]]'' in 2011 before their signing. Afterward, they began working with producer [[Keith Stegall]] on their debut album. They also toured with [[Dierks Bentley]] and [[Josh Thompson (singer)|Josh Thompson]] and released a self-titled [[extended play]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/miss-willie-brown-band-name-ep-interview/ |title=Miss Willie Brown Tell the Story of Their Band Name, Background and New EP |publisher=Tasteofcountry.com |date=2011-04-08 |accessdate=2012-07-26}}</ref> Debut single "Sick of Me" did not enter the charts, but its followup, "You're All That Matters to Me", was made into a [[music video]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theboot.com/2012/07/02/miss-willie-brown-youre-all-that-matters-to-me-video/ |title=Miss Willie Brown, 'You're All That Matters to Me' Video (Behind-the-Scenes) |publisher=The Boot |date=2012-07-02 |accessdate=2012-07-26}}</ref> and charted in August 2012.<ref>{{cite news|title=Hot Country Songs|url=http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/photos/pdf/2012/country_update_0723.pdf|accessdate=28 October 2012|newspaper=Billboard Country Update|date=23 July 2012}}</ref> Miss Willie Brown performed "You're All That Matters to Me" and "Sick of Me" on [[The CW Television Network|The CW]]'s ''[[Hart of Dixie]]'' on April 9, 2012 in the episode "[[List of Hart of Dixie episodes#Season 2 .282012.E2.80.932013.29|Always on My Mind]]".<ref>{{cite web|title=Miss Willie Brown Debuts Song On iTunes April 3, Schedules 'Hart of Dixie' Appearance|url=http://www.countrymusicislove.com/2012/03/miss-willie-brown-debuts-song-on-itunes-april-3-schedules-hart-of-dixie-appearance.html|work=Country Music Is Love|publisher=CountryMusicIsLove.com|accessdate=28 October 2012|date=30 March 2012|archive-url=https://archive.today/20130119213413/http://www.countrymusicislove.com/2012/03/miss-willie-brown-debuts-song-on-itunes-april-3-schedules-hart-of-dixie-appearance.html|archive-date=19 January 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref>

On April 16, 2013, the duo announced on their [[Facebook]] page that they were dissolving the group with Watkins pursuing a solo career and Buckley taking a break from music.
