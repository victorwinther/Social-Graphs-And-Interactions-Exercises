'''Robyn and Ryleigh''' Gillespie are a sister duo of country/pop singer-songwriters from [[Langley, British Columbia (city)|Langley]], [[British Columbia]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bclocalnews.com/entertainment/robyn-and-ryleigh-go-on-the-record/|title = Robyn and Ryleigh go on the record|date = 23 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url = https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/cbc-musical-nooners-july-29-robyn-ryleigh-1.3168427| title = CBC Musical Nooners July 29: Robyn & Ryleigh {{!}} CBC News}} </ref>

In 2013, their self-titled debut album ''Robyn and Ryleigh'' was released on Raincoast Music/[[MDM Recordings Inc.|MDM Recordings]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=About â€“ Robyn & Ryleigh |url=http://www.robynandryleigh.com/about/ |website=Robyn & Ryleigh |access-date=2015-10-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150915040921/http://www.robynandryleigh.com/about/ |archive-date=2015-09-15 |url-status=live }}</ref>

Robyn & Ryleigh were signed to [[Royalty Records]] in 2015, and have distribution through [[Sony Music Canada|Sony Music Entertainment]]. The sisters released a single with Royalty called "I Found You" on 18 May 2015.<ref>{{Cite web |title=New Music & Video From Sister Duo Robyn & Ryleigh |url=http://royaltyrecords.ca/?p=5934 |website=Royalty Records |access-date=2015-10-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150905083050/http://royaltyrecords.ca/?p=5934 |archive-date=2015-09-05 |url-status=live }}</ref>

Robyn and Ryleigh were nominated in 2015 for British Columbia Country Music Association awards (BCCMA Awards) in the categories of Group/Duo of the Year, Songwriter of the Year, and Website of the Year.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Robyn & Ryleigh Nominated in 3 Categories at This Year's BC Country Music Association Awards |url=http://royaltyrecords.ca/?p=6145 |website=Royalty Records |access-date=2015-10-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304023637/http://royaltyrecords.ca/?p=6145 |archive-date=2016-03-04 |url-status=live }}</ref>
