'''Caitlyn Elizabeth Smith'''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ascap.com/es/repertory#/ace/search/workID/883477058?page=1 |title=THE HEART OF DIXIE |website=ASCAP |publisher=American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers |access-date=September 19, 2023}}</ref> (born June 13, 1986) is an American singer-songwriter and musician. She was raised in [[Cannon Falls, Minnesota]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.elle.com/culture/music/a44994/caitlyn-smith-profile/|title=How Caitlyn Smith Defied Sex Symbol Clichés to Break Out in Nashville|date=May 2, 2017|work=ELLE|access-date=July 11, 2017|language=en}}</ref> Smith's debut album ''[[Starfire (Caitlyn Smith album)|Starfire]]'' was released on Monument Records on January 19, 2018.<ref name="auto">{{Cite magazine|title= Caitlyn Smith Steps Into the Spotlight With 'Starfire' After Penning Songs for Meghan Trainor and Garth Brooks|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8070989/caitlyn-smith-starfire-premiere|access-date=December 19, 2017}}</ref> Her second studio album, ''Supernova'', was released on March 13, 2020.<ref name="supernova"/> Her third album ''[[High & Low (Caitlyn Smith album)|High & Low]]'' was released on April 14, 2023.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Toth|first1=Emma|title=Caitlyn Smith To Release Self-Produced Third Album, 'High & Low'|url=https://countrynow.com/caitlyn-smith-to-release-self-produced-third-album-high-low/|website=Country Now|access-date=April 13, 2023|date=January 25, 2023}}</ref>

She has written songs recorded by [[Meghan Trainor]] ("[[Like I'm Gonna Lose You]]", duet with [[John Legend]], #1 on [[Adult Top 40|Billboard Adult Top 40]] chart), [[Kenny Rogers]] and [[Dolly Parton]] ("[[You Can't Make Old Friends]]"), [[Avicii]]  ("Chained") and ("Love"), [[Miley Cyrus]] ("High") and more.

She released the EP ''Starfire'' in July 2016. In January 2017, she announced she had signed a label deal with the newly reformed [[Monument Records]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Sony Music's Monument Records Revived By Jason Owen, Shane McAnally|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/7669714/monument-records-sony-music-label|website=Billboard.com|accessdate=July 11, 2017}}</ref> Smith has played at [[Lollapalooza]], Bourbon & Beyond Festival, Americana Fest and ACL Fest.<ref name="auto"/>

Caitlyn was named one of [[Rolling Stone Magazine]]'s "10 New Artists You Need To Know".<ref>{{cite web|title=10 New Country Artists You Need To Know: May 2016| date=May 4, 2016 |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/lists/10-new-country-artists-you-need-to-know-may-2016-20160504/caitlyn-smith-20160504|publisher=Rolling Stone Country|accessdate=July 11, 2017}}</ref> She recorded "The Card You Gamble", the theme song of the drama series ''[[Monarch (American TV series)|Monarch]]'', which premiered in 2022.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://entertainment-focus.com/2022/01/14/listen-caitlyn-smith-unveils-the-card-you-gamble-the-theme-from-monarch/ | title=Listen: Caitlyn Smith unveils 'The Card You Gamble', the theme from 'Monarch' | date=January 14, 2022 }}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Caitlyn lives in Nashville with her husband, songwriter Rollie Gaalswyk. They have three sons together, Thomas Miles Gaalswyk (born in 2016), Lewis James Gaalswyk (born in 2018), and Jacob Roland Gaalswyk (born in 2023).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://people.com/parents/caitlyn-smith-welcomes-son-lewis-james/|title=Her New Little 'Starfire' Has Arrived! Caitlyn Smith Introduces Son Lewis James: 'So Grateful'|website=People.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://celebritybabies.people.com/2016/09/12/caitlyn-smith-welcomes-son-thomas-miles/|title=Caitlyn Smith Welcomes Son Thomas Miles|website=People.com|access-date=July 12, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/C0PGvfOsxX6/?img_index=1|title=Welcome to the world, JACOB ROLAND GAALSWYK!|website=Instagram.com}}</ref>
