'''Alecia Nugent''' (born in Hickory Grove, Louisiana) is a [[parish music|country]], [[Americana (music)|Americana]], [[bluegrass music|bluegrass]] vocalist and musician.  Alecia has worked with bluegrass legend [[Carl Jackson]] on all of her albums.  Alecia was nominated for IBMA Female Vocalist of The Year in 2009.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.alecianugent.com/news.htm |title=Alecia Nugent&#124;Hillbilly Goddess, Rounder, Americana, Bluegrass,Country, Roots Music |access-date=September 4, 2009 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100106135916/http://www.alecianugent.com/news.htm |archive-date=January 6, 2010 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rounder.com/ibma/2009/nominations.html |title=Rounder Records 2009 IBMA Awards Nominations |website=www.rounder.com |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090915182159/http://www.rounder.com/ibma/2009/nominations.html |archive-date=2009-09-15}}</ref>

==Touring==
Nugent performed in Scarborough, Ontario, Canada in 2008 as part of the Bluegrass Sundays Winter Concert Series.<ref>''Strings'', newsletter of the Pineridge Bluegrass Folklore Society, December 2007</ref>
