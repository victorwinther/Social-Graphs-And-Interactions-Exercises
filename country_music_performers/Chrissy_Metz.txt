'''Christine Michelle Metz''' (born September 29, 1980) is an  American actress and singer. She played [[List of This Is Us characters#Kate Pearson|Kate Pearson]] in the television series ''[[This Is Us]]'' (2016–2022), which earned her nominations for a [[Primetime Emmy Award]] and two [[Golden Globe Awards]]. She has also appeared in films such as ''[[Sierra Burgess Is a Loser]]'' (2018) and ''[[Breakthrough (2019 film)|Breakthrough]]'' (2019).

==Early life==
Metz was born in [[Homestead, Florida|Homestead]], [[Florida]], to Denise and Mark Metz<ref name=birthdate /> on September 29, 1980.<ref>{{cite book |title=Gale Biography Online Collection |date=2016 |publisher=Gale |url=https://link.gale.com/apps/doc/K1650010574/BIC?u=wikipedia&sid=bookmark-BIC&xid=4a698fe5 |access-date=October 6, 2022 |format=Collection |chapter=Chrissy Metz}}</ref> She spent her early years in [[Japan]], where her father was stationed by the US Navy.<ref name=birthdate /> The family later moved to [[Gainesville, Florida]], where she attended elementary, middle, and high school.<ref name=birthdate>{{cite web |url=http://www.glamour.com/story/this-is-us-star-chrissy-metz-talks-plus-size-labels |title='This Is Us' Star Chrissy Metz Talks Plus-Size Labels: 'I'm So Much More Than the Weight I Carry on My Body.' |first=Jessica |last=Radloff |work=[[Glamour (magazine)|Glamour]] |date=September 21, 2016 |access-date=January 25, 2017 |quote=[Metz] turned 36 on September 29, days after [''This Is Us''] aired its second episode ... ==Career==
She played Kate Pearson in the [[NBC]] drama series ''[[This Is Us (TV series)|This Is Us]]'', which earned her [[Primetime Emmy Award]] and [[Golden Globe Award]] nominations. She played [[Barbara (American Horror Story)|Ima Wiggles]] in FX's ''[[American Horror Story: Freak Show]]''.<ref>{{cite magazine |title='This Is Us': Before They Were Stars |url=https://www.ew.com/gallery/this-us-before-they-were-stars/2748573_chrissy-metz |first=Madeline |last=Boardman |magazine=[[Entertainment Weekly]] |date=September 14, 2016 |access-date=September 22, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title='This is Us' Actress Chrissy Metz on Her Plus-Sized Role: This is a 'Story That Needs to Be Told' |url=https://variety.com/2016/tv/news/this-is-us-chrissy-metz-sterling-k-brown-mandy-moore-1201860347/ |first=Maria |last=Cavassuto |work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |date=September 14, 2016 |access-date=September 22, 2016}}</ref>

Metz is a singer in her band Chrissy and the Vapors.<ref name= "singing">{{cite news |last=Maple |first=Taylor |url=https://www.bustle.com/p/videos-of-chrissy-metz-singing-before-this-is-us-reveal-that-kates-musical-future-couldnt-be-in-better-hands-2759943 |title=Videos Of Chrissy Metz Singing Before 'This Is Us' Reveal That Kate's Musical Future Couldn't Be In Better Hands |work=[[Bustle (magazine)|Bustle]] |date=October 3, 2017 |access-date=April 15, 2018}}</ref>

In early 2018, it was announced that Metz would star in ''[[Breakthrough (2019 film)|Breakthrough]]'', working with producer [[DeVon Franklin]].<ref>{{cite web |title='This Is Us' Star Chrissy Metz Set To Topline 'The Impossible' For Fox 2000 |url=https://deadline.com/2018/01/this-is-us-chrissy-metz-the-impossible-1202275823/ |first=Amanda |last=N'Duka |website=[[Deadline Hollywood]] |date=January 31, 2018 |access-date=March 6, 2018}}</ref> She is cast as Joyce Smith, the mother of John, a 14-year-old boy who fell through an icy [[Lake St. Louis, Missouri|Missouri lake]] and was proclaimed dead. The movie, based on the book written by Smith, explores the mother's belief that her son was brought back to life by God interceding through her and other's prayers. In 2021, Metz portrayed the ghost of Haunting Harriet in ''[[Muppets Haunted Mansion]]''.

Metz studies with acting coach John Kirby.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.broadwayworld.com/los-angeles/article/A-STEADY-RAIN-Extends-After-Sold-Out-Hollywood-Fringe-Festival-Run-20170630 |title=A STEADY RAIN Extends After Sold-Out Hollywood Fringe Festival Run |website=BroadwayWorld.com |access-date=April 18, 2019}}</ref>

In 2021, Chrissy Metz was a guest panelist in [[The Masked Singer (American TV series) season 5|season five]] of ''[[The Masked Singer (American TV series)|The Masked Singer]]'' where she appeared in its eighth episode.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://ew.com/tv/rita-wilson-chrissy-metz-join-the-masked-singer/|title=Rita Wilson and Chrissy Metz to join The Masked Singer as guest panelists|website=Entertainment Weekly|first=Lauren|last=Huff|date=April 6, 2021|access-date=May 29, 2024}}</ref> In 2024, Metz competed in [[The Masked Singer (American TV series) season 11|season eleven]] as "Poodle Moth". After losing to contestant Thelma Houston as "Clock" in the knockoffs on "Shower Anthem Night", she was saved by [[Rita Ora]] who rang the Ding Dong Keep It On Bell due to the chanting of the audience. Metz was eliminated during the quarterfinals.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2024/tv/news/the-masked-singer-season-11-episode-10-recap-chrissy-metz-poodle-moth-1235995423/|title='The Masked Singer' Reveals Identity of the Poodle Moth: Here’s the Celebrity Under the Costume |website=Variety|first=Michael|last=Schneider|date=May 8, 2024|access-date=May 9, 2024}}</ref>

In November 2023, Metz was a guest narrator at [[Disney's Candlelight Processional]] at [[Walt Disney World]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.orlandosentinel.com/2023/10/05/disney-epcot-candlelight-processional-new-narrators-2023/ |title=Disney: 9 new narrators set for Candlelight Processional |date=October 5, 2023 |access-date=February 19, 2024}}</ref> Metz was also a performer on the 2023 Wonderful World of Disney: Magical Holiday Celebration television special.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://disneyparks.disney.go.com/blog/2023/11/disney-parks-and-abc-reveal-stars-schedule-for-2023-holiday-tv-specials/ |title=Disney Parks and ABC Reveal Stars, Schedule for 2023 Holiday TV Specials |date=November 16, 2023 |website=Disney Parks Blog |access-date=February 19, 2024}}</ref> In December of the same year, she sang "[[Silver Bells]]" in the holiday television special ''Christmas at the Opry."<ref>{{cite news |last1=Manno |first1=Jackie |title=All the Celebrities Attending Christmas at the Opry |url=https://www.nbc.com/nbc-insider/all-the-celebrities-attending-christmas-at-the-opry |access-date=December 8, 2023 |work=NBC Insider Official Site |date=December 6, 2023}}</ref>

==Personal life==
On January 5, 2008, Metz married British journalist Martyn Eaden at the courthouse in [[Santa Barbara, California]].<ref name=divorce>{{cite web |title=Car, Cash & Courtroom Snub: Inside 'This Is Us' Star Chrissy Metz's Dirty Divorce From Ex |url=http://radaronline.com/celebrity-news/chrissy-metz-divorce-file-martyn-eaden-this-is-us/ |first=Teresa |last=Roca |work=Radar Online |date=February 14, 2017}}</ref> They separated in January 2013, and Eaden filed for divorce from Metz in November 2014, citing "irreconcilable differences."<ref>{{cite web |title=How Chrissy Metz's 'First Real Love' Broke Her Heart In Secret Divorce |url=http://radaronline.com/celebrity-news/chrissy-metz-husband-martyn-eaden-files-divorce/ |work=Radar Online |date=February 1, 2017}}</ref> On December 11, 2015, their divorce was finalized.

From 2016 to 2018, she dated Josh Stancil, who was a cameraman for ''This Is Us''.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Hearon |first=Sarah |date=March 30, 2018 |title=Chrissy Metz and Josh Stancil Call It Quits: 'I'm Dating Myself Currently' |url=https://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/chrissy-metz-and-boyfriend-josh-stancil-split/ |access-date=March 4, 2023 |website=Us Weekly |language=en-US}}</ref> She dated composer Hal Rosenfeld from 2018 to 2020.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Ballhaus |first=Louisa |date=August 21, 2020 |title=Chrissy Metz Has Called It Quits with Longtime Boyfriend Hal Rosenfeld |url=https://www.sheknows.com/entertainment/articles/2327285/chrissy-metz-hal-rosenfeld-breakup/ |access-date=March 4, 2023 |website=SheKnows |language=en-US}}</ref>

From 2020 to 2023, she dated songwriter and former music executive Bradley Collins after meeting him on the dating app [[Bumble]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=February 15, 2023 |title=Why Chrissy Metz Says She Had to 'Beg' For Her Boyfriend to Ask For Her Number |url=https://www.nbc.com/nbc-insider/chrissy-metz-had-to-beg-her-boyfriend-to-ask-for-her-number |access-date=March 4, 2023 |website=NBC Insider Official Site |language=en-US}}</ref> They co-wrote a children's book ''When I Talk to God, I Talk About You''.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.etonline.com/chrissy-metz-and-boyfriend-bradley-collins-recall-the-origins-of-their-pandemic-romance-exclusive |title=Chrissy Metz and Boyfriend Bradley Collins Recall the Origins of Their Pandemic Romance (Exclusive) |publisher=ET |date=February 14, 2023 |access-date=February 14, 2023}}</ref>

Metz is a Christian.<ref>{{cite web |date=April 17, 2019 |title='Breakthrough' impacted 'This Is Us' star Chrissy Metz's own faith |url=https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/breakthrough-movie-chrissy-metz-faith |access-date=August 22, 2020 |publisher=Fox News}}</ref>
